# 1 Chronicles, Chapter 6

### Chapter 6

1 The sons of Levi were Gershon, Kohath, and Merari. ^1-chronicles-06-1

2 The sons of Gershon were named Libni and Shimei. ^1-chronicles-06-2

3 The sons of Kohath were Amram, Izhar, Hebron, and Uzziel. ^1-chronicles-06-3

4 The sons of Merari were Mahli and Mushi. ^1-chronicles-06-4

These were the clans of Levi, according to their ancestors.

5 Of Gershon: his son Libni, whose son was Jahath, whose son was Zimmah, ^1-chronicles-06-5

6 whose son was Joah, whose son was Iddo, whose son was Zerah, whose son was Jetherai. ^1-chronicles-06-6

7 The sons of Kohath: his son Amminadab, whose son was Korah, whose son was Assir, ^1-chronicles-06-7

8 whose son was Elkanah, whose son was Ebiasaph, whose son was Assir, ^1-chronicles-06-8

9 whose son was Tahath, whose son was Uriel, whose son was Uzziah, whose son was Shaul. ^1-chronicles-06-9

10 The sons of Elkanah were Amasai and Ahimoth, ^1-chronicles-06-10

11 whose son was Elkanah, whose son was Zophai, whose son was Nahath, ^1-chronicles-06-11

12 whose son was Eliab, whose son was Jeroham, whose son was Elkanah, whose son was Samuel. ^1-chronicles-06-12

13 The sons of Samuel were Joel, the firstborn, and Abijah, the second. ^1-chronicles-06-13

14 The sons of Merari: Mahli, whose son was Libni, whose son was Shimei, whose son was Uzzah, ^1-chronicles-06-14

15 whose son was Shimea, whose son was Haggiah, whose son was Asaiah. ^1-chronicles-06-15

16 The following were established by David for the service of song in the L ORD’s house at the time when the ark had a resting place. ^1-chronicles-06-16

17 They served as singers before the tabernacle of the tent of meeting until Solomon built the house of the L ORD in Jerusalem, and they performed their services according to the order prescribed for them. ^1-chronicles-06-17

18 Those who so performed are the following, together with their sons. ^1-chronicles-06-18

Among the Kohathites: Heman, the chanter, son of Joel, son of Samuel,

19 son of Elkanah, son of Jeroham, son of Eliel, son of Toah, ^1-chronicles-06-19

20 son of Zuph, son of Elkanah, son of Mahath, son of Amasi, ^1-chronicles-06-20

21 son of Elkanah, son of Joel, son of Azariah, son of Zephaniah, ^1-chronicles-06-21

22 son of Tahath, son of Assir, son of Ebiasaph, son of Korah, ^1-chronicles-06-22

23 son of Izhar, son of Kohath, son of Levi, son of Israel. ^1-chronicles-06-23

24 His brother Asaph stood at his right hand. Asaph was the son of Berechiah, son of Shimea, ^1-chronicles-06-24

25 son of Michael, son of Baaseiah, son of Malchijah, ^1-chronicles-06-25

26 son of Ethni, son of Zerah, son of Adaiah, ^1-chronicles-06-26

27 son of Ethan, son of Zimmah, son of Shimei, ^1-chronicles-06-27

28 son of Jahath, son of Gershon, son of Levi. ^1-chronicles-06-28

29 Their brothers, the Merarites, stood at the left: Ethan, son of Kishi, son of Abdi, son of Malluch, ^1-chronicles-06-29

30 son of Hashabiah, son of Amaziah, son of Hilkiah, ^1-chronicles-06-30

31 son of Amzi, son of Bani, son of Shemer, ^1-chronicles-06-31

32 son of Mahli, son of Mushi, son of Merari, son of Levi. ^1-chronicles-06-32

33 Their brother Levites were appointed to all the other services of the tabernacle of the house of God. ^1-chronicles-06-33

34 However, it was Aaron and his sons who made the sacrifice on the altar for burnt offerings and on the altar of incense; they alone had charge of the holy of holies and of making atonement for Israel, as Moses, the servant of God, had commanded. ^1-chronicles-06-34

35 These were the sons of Aaron: his son Eleazar, whose son was Phinehas, whose son was Abishua, ^1-chronicles-06-35

36 whose son was Bukki, whose son was Uzzi, whose son was Zerahiah, ^1-chronicles-06-36

37 whose son was Meraioth, whose son was Amariah, whose son was Ahitub, ^1-chronicles-06-37

38 whose son was Zadok, whose son was Ahimaaz. ^1-chronicles-06-38

39 The following were their dwelling places, by encampments in their territories. To the sons of Aaron who belonged to the clan of the Kohathites, since the lot fell to them, ^1-chronicles-06-39

40 was assigned Hebron in the land of Judah, with its adjacent pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-40

41 However, the open country and the villages belonging to the city had been given to Caleb, the son of Jephunneh. ^1-chronicles-06-41

42 There were assigned to the sons of Aaron: Hebron a city of refuge, Libnah with its pasture lands, Jattir with its pasture lands, Eshtemoa with its pasture lands, ^1-chronicles-06-42

43 Holon with its pasture lands, Debir with its pasture lands, ^1-chronicles-06-43

44 Ashan with its pasture lands, Jetta with its pasture lands, and Beth-shemesh with its pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-44

45 Also from the tribe of Benjamin: Gibeon with its pasture lands, Geba with its pasture lands, Almon with its pasture lands, Anathoth with its pasture lands. In all, they had thirteen cities with their pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-45

49 The Israelites assigned these cities with their pasture lands to the Levites, ^1-chronicles-06-49

50 designating them by name and assigning them by lot from the tribes of the Judahites, Simeonites, and Benjaminites. ^1-chronicles-06-50

46 The other Kohathites obtained ten cities by lot for their clans from the tribe of Ephraim, from the tribe of Dan, and from the half-tribe of Manasseh. ^1-chronicles-06-46

47 The clans of the Gershonites obtained thirteen cities from the tribes of Issachar, Asher, and Naphtali, and from the half-tribe of Manasseh in Bashan. ^1-chronicles-06-47

48 The clans of the Merarites obtained twelve cities by lot from the tribes of Reuben, Gad, and Zebulun. ^1-chronicles-06-48

51 The clans of the Kohathites obtained cities by lot from the tribe of Ephraim. ^1-chronicles-06-51

52 They were assigned cities of refuge: Shechem in the mountain region of Ephraim, with its pasture lands, Gezer with its pasture lands, ^1-chronicles-06-52

53 Kibzaim with its pasture lands, and Beth-horon with its pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-53

54 From the tribe of Dan: Elteke with its pasture lands, Gibbethon with its pasture lands, Aijalon with its pasture lands, and Gath-rimmon with its pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-54

55 From the half-tribe of Manasseh: Taanach with its pasture lands and Ibleam with its pasture lands. These belonged to the rest of the Kohathite clan. ^1-chronicles-06-55

56 The clans of the Gershonites received from the half-tribe of Manasseh: Golan in Bashan with its pasture lands and Ashtaroth with its pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-56

57 From the tribe of Issachar: Kedesh with its pasture lands, Daberath with its pasture lands, ^1-chronicles-06-57

58 Ramoth with its pasture lands, and Engannim with its pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-58

59 From the tribe of Asher: Mashal with its pasture lands, Abdon with its pasture lands, ^1-chronicles-06-59

60 Hilkath with its pasture lands, and Rehob with its pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-60

61 From the tribe of Naphtali: Kedesh in Galilee with its pasture lands, Hammon with its pasture lands, and Kiriathaim with its pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-61

62 The rest of the Merarites received from the tribe of Zebulun: Jokneam with its pasture lands, Kartah with its pasture lands, Rimmon with its pasture lands, and Tabor with its pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-62

63 Across the Jordan at Jericho (that is, east of the Jordan) they received from the tribe of Reuben: Bezer in the desert with its pasture lands, Jahzah with its pasture lands, ^1-chronicles-06-63

64 Kedemoth with its pasture lands, and Mephaath with its pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-64

65 From the tribe of Gad: Ramoth in Gilead with its pasture lands, Mahanaim with its pasture lands, ^1-chronicles-06-65

66 Heshbon with its pasture lands, and Jazer with its pasture lands. ^1-chronicles-06-66

* [6:16] – [32] The cultic functions performed by the levitical families in the postexilic Temple at the time of the Chronicler are here traced back to David, in a way analogous to that in which all the laws in the Pentateuch are attributed to Moses.
* [6:39] – [66] For the rights of the Levites in the cities assigned to them, see note on [Jos 21:1].
