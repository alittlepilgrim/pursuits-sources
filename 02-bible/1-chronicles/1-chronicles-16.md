# 1 Chronicles, Chapter 16

### Chapter 16

1 They brought in the ark of God and set it within the tent which David had pitched for it. Then they sacrificed burnt offerings and communion offerings to God. ^1-chronicles-16-1

2 When David had finished sacrificing the burnt offerings and communion offerings, he blessed the people in the name of the L ORD, ^1-chronicles-16-2

3 and distributed to every Israelite, to every man and every woman, a loaf of bread, a piece of meat, and a raisin cake. ^1-chronicles-16-3

**David’s Directives for the Levites.**

4 He then appointed certain Levites to minister before the ark of the L ORD, to celebrate, thank, and praise the L ORD, the God of Israel. ^1-chronicles-16-4

5 Asaph was their chief, and second to him were Zechariah, Uzziel, Shemiramoth, Jehiel, Mattithiah, Eliab, Benaiah, Obed-edom, and Jeiel. These were to play on harps and lyres, while Asaph was to sound the cymbals, ^1-chronicles-16-5

6 and the priests Benaiah and Jahaziel were to be the regular trumpeters before the ark of the covenant of God. ^1-chronicles-16-6

7 On that same day, David appointed Asaph and his brothers to sing for the first time these praises of the L ORD: ^1-chronicles-16-7

8 Give thanks to the L ORD, invoke his name; ^1-chronicles-16-8

make known among the peoples his deeds.

9 Sing praise, play music; ^1-chronicles-16-9

proclaim all his wondrous deeds.

10 Glory in his holy name; ^1-chronicles-16-10

rejoice, O hearts that seek the L ORD!

11 Rely on the mighty L ORD; ^1-chronicles-16-11

constantly seek his face.

12 Recall the wondrous deeds he has done, ^1-chronicles-16-12

his signs, and his words of judgment,

13 You sons of Israel, his servants, ^1-chronicles-16-13

offspring of Jacob, the chosen ones!

14 The L ORD is our God; ^1-chronicles-16-14

who rules the whole earth.

15 He remembers forever his covenant ^1-chronicles-16-15

the pact imposed for a thousand generations—

16 Which was made with [[abraham|Abraham]], ^1-chronicles-16-16

confirmed by oath to Isaac,

17 And ratified as binding for Jacob, ^1-chronicles-16-17

an everlasting covenant for Israel:

18 “To you will I give the land of Canaan, ^1-chronicles-16-18

your own allotted heritage.”

19 When they were few in number, ^1-chronicles-16-19

a handful, and strangers there,

20 Wandering from nation to nation, ^1-chronicles-16-20

from one kingdom to another,

21 He let no one oppress them; ^1-chronicles-16-21

for their sake he rebuked kings:

22 “Do not touch my anointed, ^1-chronicles-16-22

to my prophets do no harm.”

23 Sing to the L ORD, all the earth, ^1-chronicles-16-23

announce his salvation, day after day.

24 Tell his glory among the nations; ^1-chronicles-16-24

among all peoples, his wondrous deeds.

25 For great is the L ORD and highly to be praised; ^1-chronicles-16-25

to be feared above all gods.

26 For the gods of the nations all do nothing, ^1-chronicles-16-26

but the L ORD made the heavens.

27 Splendor and majesty go before him; ^1-chronicles-16-27

power and rejoicing are in his holy place.

28 Give to the L ORD, you families of nations, ^1-chronicles-16-28

give to the L ORD glory and might;

29 Give to the L ORD the glory due his name! ^1-chronicles-16-29

Bring gifts, and come before him;

bow down to the L ORD, splendid in holiness.

30 Tremble before him, all the earth; ^1-chronicles-16-30

the world will surely stand fast, never to be moved.

31 Let the heavens be glad and the earth rejoice; ^1-chronicles-16-31

let them say among the nations: The L ORD is king.

32 Let the sea and what fills it resound; ^1-chronicles-16-32

let the plains be joyful and all that is in them!

33 Then let all the trees of the forest exult ^1-chronicles-16-33

before the L ORD, who comes,

who comes to rule the earth.

34 Give thanks to the L ORD, who is good, ^1-chronicles-16-34

whose love endures forever;

35 And say, “Save us, O God, our savior, ^1-chronicles-16-35

gather us and deliver us from among the nations,

That we may give thanks to your holy name

and glory in praising you.”

36 Blessed be the L ORD, the God of Israel, ^1-chronicles-16-36

from everlasting to everlasting!

Let all the people say, Amen! Hallelujah.

37 Then David left Asaph and his brothers there before the ark of the covenant of the L ORD to minister before the ark regularly according to the daily ritual; ^1-chronicles-16-37

38 he also left there Obed-edom and sixty-eight of his brothers, including Obed-edom, son of Jeduthun, and Hosah, to be gatekeepers. ^1-chronicles-16-38

39 But the priest Zadok and his priestly brothers he left before the tabernacle of the L ORD on the high place at Gibeon, ^1-chronicles-16-39

40 to make burnt offerings to the L ORD on the altar for burnt offerings regularly, morning and evening, and to do all that is written in the law of the L ORD which he commanded Israel. ^1-chronicles-16-40

41 With them were Heman and Jeduthun and the others who were chosen and designated by name to give thanks to the L ORD, “whose love endures forever,” ^1-chronicles-16-41

42 with trumpets and cymbals for accompaniment, and instruments for sacred song. The sons of Jeduthun kept the gate. ^1-chronicles-16-42

43 Then all the people departed, each to their own homes, and David returned to bless his household. ^1-chronicles-16-43
