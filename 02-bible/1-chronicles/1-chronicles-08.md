# 1 Chronicles, Chapter 8

### Chapter 8

**Benjamin.**

1 Benjamin became the father of Bela, his firstborn, Ashbel, the second son, Aharah, the third, ^1-chronicles-08-1

2 Nohah, the fourth, and Rapha, the fifth. ^1-chronicles-08-2

3 The sons of Bela were Addar and Gera, the father of Ehud. ^1-chronicles-08-3

4 The sons of Ehud were Abishua, Naaman, Ahoah, ^1-chronicles-08-4

5 Gera, Shephuphan, and Huram. ^1-chronicles-08-5

6 These were the sons of Ehud, family heads over those who dwelt in Geba and were deported to Manahath. ^1-chronicles-08-6

7 Also Naaman, Ahijah, and Gera. The last, who led them into exile, became the father of Uzza and Ahihud. ^1-chronicles-08-7

8 Shaharaim became a father on the Moabite plateau after he had put away his wives Hushim and Baara. ^1-chronicles-08-8

9 By his wife Hodesh he begot Jobab, Zibia, Mesha, Malcam, ^1-chronicles-08-9

10 Jeuz, Sachia, and Mirmah. These were his sons, family heads. ^1-chronicles-08-10

11 By Hushim he begot Abitub and Elpaal. ^1-chronicles-08-11

12 The sons of Elpaal were Eber, Misham, Shemed (who built Ono and Lod with its nearby towns), ^1-chronicles-08-12

13 and Beriah, and Shema. They were family heads of those who dwelt in Aijalon, and they put the inhabitants of Gath to flight. ^1-chronicles-08-13

14 Their relatives were Elpaal, Shashak, and Jeremoth. ^1-chronicles-08-14

15 Zebadiah, Arad, Eder, ^1-chronicles-08-15

16 Michael, Ishpah, and Joha were the sons of Beriah. ^1-chronicles-08-16

17 Zebadiah, Meshullam, Hizki, Heber, ^1-chronicles-08-17

18 Ishmerai, Izliah, and Jobab were the sons of Elpaal. ^1-chronicles-08-18

19 Jakim, Zichri, Zabdi, ^1-chronicles-08-19

20 Elienai, Zillethai, Eliel, ^1-chronicles-08-20

21 Adaiah, Beraiah, and Shimrath were the sons of Shimei. ^1-chronicles-08-21

22 Ishpan, Eber, Eliel, ^1-chronicles-08-22

23 Abdon, Zichri, Hanan, ^1-chronicles-08-23

24 Hananiah, Elam, Anthothijah, ^1-chronicles-08-24

25 Iphdeiah, and Penuel were the sons of Shashak. ^1-chronicles-08-25

26 Shamsherai, Shehariah, Athaliah, ^1-chronicles-08-26

27 Jaareshiah, Elijah, and Zichri were the sons of Jeroham. ^1-chronicles-08-27

28 These were family heads in their generations, chiefs who dwelt in Jerusalem. ^1-chronicles-08-28

29 In Gibeon dwelt Jeiel, the founder of Gibeon, whose wife’s name was Maacah; ^1-chronicles-08-29

30 also his firstborn son, Abdon, and Zur, Kish, Baal, Ner, Nadab, ^1-chronicles-08-30

31 Gedor, Ahio, Zecher, and Mikloth. ^1-chronicles-08-31

32 Mikloth became the father of Shimeah. These, too, dwelt with their relatives in Jerusalem, opposite their fellow tribesmen. ^1-chronicles-08-32

33 Ner became the father of Kish, and Kish became the father of Saul. Saul became the father of Jonathan, Malchishua, Abinadab, and Eshbaal. ^1-chronicles-08-33

34 The son of Jonathan was Meribbaal, and Meribbaal became the father of Micah. ^1-chronicles-08-34

35 The sons of Micah were Pithon, Melech, Tarea, and Ahaz. ^1-chronicles-08-35

36 Ahaz became the father of Jehoaddah, and Jehoaddah became the father of Alemeth, Azmaveth, and Zimri. Zimri became the father of Moza. ^1-chronicles-08-36

37 Moza became the father of Binea, whose son was Raphah, whose son was Eleasah, whose son was Azel. ^1-chronicles-08-37

38 Azel had six sons, whose names were Azrikam, his firstborn, Ishmael, Sheariah, Azariah, Obadiah, and Hanan; all these were the sons of Azel. ^1-chronicles-08-38

39 The sons of Eshek, his brother, were Ulam, his firstborn, Jeush, the second son, and Eliphelet, the third. ^1-chronicles-08-39

40 The sons of Ulam were warriors, skilled with the bow, and they had many sons and grandsons: one hundred and fifty. All these were the sons of Benjamin. ^1-chronicles-08-40
