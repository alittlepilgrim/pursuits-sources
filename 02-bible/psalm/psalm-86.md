# Psalm 86

## Prayer in Time of Distress

1 A prayer of David. ^psalm-86-1

### I

Incline your ear, LORD, and answer me,

for I am poor and oppressed.

2 Preserve my life, for I am devoted; ^psalm-86-2

save your servant who trusts in you.

You are my God;

3 be gracious to me, Lord; ^psalm-86-3

to you I call all the day.

4 Gladden the soul of your servant; ^psalm-86-4

to you, Lord, I lift up my soul.

5 Lord, you are good and forgiving, ^psalm-86-5

most merciful to all who call on you.

6 LORD, hear my prayer; ^psalm-86-6

listen to my cry for help.

7 On the day of my distress I call to you, ^psalm-86-7

for you will answer me.

### II

8 None among the gods can equal you, O Lord; ^psalm-86-8

nor can their deeds compare to yours.

9 All the nations you have made shall come ^psalm-86-9

to bow before you, Lord,

and give honor to your name.

10 For you are great and do wondrous deeds; ^psalm-86-10

and you alone are God.

### III

11 Teach me, LORD, your way ^psalm-86-11

that I may walk in your truth,

single-hearted and revering your name.

12 I will praise you with all my heart, ^psalm-86-12

glorify your name forever, Lord my God.

13 Your mercy to me is great; ^psalm-86-13

you have rescued me from the depths of Sheol.

14 O God, the arrogant have risen against me; ^psalm-86-14

a ruthless band has sought my life;

to you they pay no heed.

15 But you, Lord, are a compassionate and gracious God, ^psalm-86-15

slow to anger, abounding in mercy and truth.

16 Turn to me, be gracious to me; ^psalm-86-16

give your strength to your servant;

save the son of your handmaid.

17 Give me a sign of your favor: ^psalm-86-17

make my enemies see, to their confusion,

that you, LORD, help and comfort me.
