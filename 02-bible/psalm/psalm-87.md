# Psalm 87

## Zion the True Birthplace

1 A psalm of the Korahites. A song. ^psalm-87-1

### I

His foundation is on holy mountains,

2 The LORD loves the gates of Zion ^psalm-87-2

more than any dwelling in Jacob.

3 Glorious things are said of you, ^psalm-87-3

O city of God!

*Selah*

### II

4 Rahab and Babylon I count ^psalm-87-4

among those who know me.

See, Philistia and Tyre, with Ethiopia,

“This one was born there.”

5 And of Zion it will be said: ^psalm-87-5

“Each one was born in it.”

The Most High will establish it;

“This one was born there.”

*Selah*

7 So singers and dancers: ^psalm-87-7

“All my springs are in you.”

\* (Psalm 87) A song of Zion, like [Ps 46]; [48]; [76]; [132].

\* (87:2) The gates: the city itself, a common Hebrew idiom.

\* (87:5] – [6) The bond between the exile and the holy city was so strong as to override the exile’s citizenship of lesser cities.

b. (87:5) [Gal 4:26].

d. (87:6) [Is 4:3].

e. (87:7) [Ps 68:26]; [149:3].
