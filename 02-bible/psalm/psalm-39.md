### Psalm 39

### The Vanity of Life

1 For the leader, for Jeduthun. A psalm of David. ^psalm-39-1

#### I

2 I said, “I will watch my ways, ^psalm-39-2

lest I sin with my tongue;

I will keep a muzzle on my mouth.”

3 Mute and silent before the wicked, ^psalm-39-3

I refrain from good things.

But my sorrow increases;

4 my heart smolders within me. ^psalm-39-4

In my sighing a fire blazes up,

and I break into speech:

#### Ii

5 L ORD, let me know my end, the number of my days, ^psalm-39-5

that I may learn how frail I am.

6 To be sure, you establish the expanse of my days; ^psalm-39-6

indeed, my life is as nothing before you.

Every man is but a breath.

*Selah*

#### Iii

7 Man goes about as a mere phantom; ^psalm-39-7

they hurry about, although in vain;

he heaps up stores without knowing for whom.

8 And now, L ORD, for what do I wait? ^psalm-39-8

You are my only hope.

9 From all my sins deliver me; ^psalm-39-9

let me not be the taunt of fools.

10 I am silent and do not open my mouth ^psalm-39-10

because you are the one who did this.

11 Take your plague away from me; ^psalm-39-11

I am ravaged by the touch of your hand.

12 You chastise man with rebukes for sin; ^psalm-39-12

like a moth you consume his treasures.

Every man is but a breath.

*Selah*

13 Listen to my prayer, L ORD, hear my cry; ^psalm-39-13

do not be deaf to my weeping!

For I am with you like a foreigner,

a refugee, like my ancestors.

14 Turn your gaze from me, that I may smile ^psalm-39-14

before I depart to be no more.

\* (Psalm 39) The lament of a mortally ill person who at first had resolved to remain silently submissive ([Ps 39:2] – [4]). But the grief was too much and now the psalmist laments the brevity and vanity of life ([Ps 39:5] – [7]), yet remaining hopeful ([Ps 39:8] – [10]). The psalmist continues to express both acceptance of the illness and hope for healing in [Ps 39:11] – [13].

b. (39:4) [Jer 20:9].

c. (39:6) [Ps 62:10]; [90:9] – [10]; [144:4]; [Jb 7:6], [16]; [14:1], [5]; [Eccl 6:12]; ([[wisdom-02|Wis 2]]:5].

d. (39:13) [Ps 119:19]; [[genesis-23|Gen 23]]:4; [Heb 11:13]; [1 Pt 2:11].
