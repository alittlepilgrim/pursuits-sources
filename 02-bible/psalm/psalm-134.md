### Psalm 134

### Exhortation to the Night Watch to Bless God

1 A song of ascents.

O come, bless the L ORD,

all you servants of the L ORD

You who stand in the house of the L ORD

throughout the nights.

2 Lift up your hands toward the sanctuary,

and bless the L ORD.

3 May the L ORD bless you from Zion,

the Maker of heaven and earth.

\* (Psalm 134) A brief liturgy exhorting all those who serve in the Jerusalem Temple during the night (cf. [Is 30:29]) to praise God with words and gestures. Although he is the Creator of the whole universe, God’s blessings emanate in a unique way from Zion, the city of Jerusalem.

\* (134:1) Servants of the L ORD: priests and Levites, cf. [Dt 10:8]; [Ps 113:1]; [135:1]; [Dn 3:85].

a. (134:1) [Ps 135:1] – [2]; [1 Chr 9:33].

b. (134:2) ([[psalm-28|Ps 28]]:2]; [141:2].

c. (134:3) ([[psalm-20|Ps 20]]:3]; [128:5]; [Nm 6:24].
