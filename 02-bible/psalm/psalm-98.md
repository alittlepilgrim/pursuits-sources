# Psalm 98

## The Coming of God

1 A psalm. ^psalm-98-1

### I

Sing a new song to the LORD,

for he has done marvelous deeds.

His right hand and holy arm

have won the victory.

2 The LORD has made his victory known; ^psalm-98-2

has revealed his triumph in the sight of the nations,

3 He has remembered his mercy and faithfulness ^psalm-98-3

toward the house of Israel.

All the ends of the earth have seen

the victory of our God.

### II

4 Shout with joy to the LORD, all the earth; ^psalm-98-4

break into song; sing praise.

5 Sing praise to the LORD with the lyre, ^psalm-98-5

with the lyre and melodious song.

6 With trumpets and the sound of the horn ^psalm-98-6

shout with joy to the King, the LORD.

### III

7 Let the sea and what fills it resound, ^psalm-98-7

the world and those who dwell there.

8 Let the rivers clap their hands, ^psalm-98-8

the mountains shout with them for joy,

9 Before the LORD who comes, ^psalm-98-9

who comes to govern the earth,

To govern the world with justice

and the peoples with fairness.

\* (Psalm 98) A hymn, similar to [Ps 96], extolling God for Israel’s victory ([Ps 98:1] – [3]). All nations ([Ps 98:4] – [6]) and even inanimate nature ([Ps 98:7] – [8]) are summoned to welcome God’s coming to rule over the world ([Ps 98:9]).
