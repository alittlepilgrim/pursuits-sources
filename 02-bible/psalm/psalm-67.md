# Psalm 67

## Harvest Thanks and Petition

1 For the leader; with stringed instruments. A psalm; a song. ^psalm-67-1

### I

2 May God be gracious to us and bless us; ^psalm-67-2

may his face shine upon us.

*Selah*

3 So shall your way be known upon the earth, ^psalm-67-3

your victory among all the nations.

4 May the peoples praise you, God; ^psalm-67-4

may all the peoples praise you!

### II

5 May the nations be glad and rejoice; ^psalm-67-5

for you judge the peoples with fairness,

you guide the nations upon the earth.

*Selah*

6 May the peoples praise you, God; ^psalm-67-6

may all the peoples praise you!

### III

7 The earth has yielded its harvest; ^psalm-67-7

God, our God, blesses us.

8 May God bless us still; ^psalm-67-8

that the ends of the earth may revere him.

\* (Psalm 67) A petition for a bountiful harvest ([Ps 67:7]), made in the awareness that Israel’s prosperity will persuade the nations to worship its God.

\* (67:2) May God be gracious to us: the people’s petition echoes the blessing pronounced upon them by the priests, cf. [Nm 6:22] – [27].
