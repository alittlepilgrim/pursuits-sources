### Psalm 53

### A Lament over Widespread Corruption

1 For the leader; according to *Mahalath*. A *maskil* of David. ^psalm-53-1

#### I

2 The fool says in his heart, ^psalm-53-2

“There is no God.”

They act corruptly and practice injustice;

there is none that does good.

3 God looks out from the heavens ^psalm-53-3

upon the children of Adam,

To see if there is a discerning person

who is seeking God.

4 All have gone astray; ^psalm-53-4

each one is altogether perverse.

There is not one who does what is good, not even one.

#### Ii

5 Do they not know better, those who do evil, ^psalm-53-5

who feed upon my people as they feed upon bread?

Have they not called upon God?

6 They are going to fear his name with great fear, ^psalm-53-6

though they had not feared it before.

For God will scatter the bones

of those encamped against you.

They will surely be put to shame,

for God has rejected them.

#### Iii

7 Who will bring forth from Zion ^psalm-53-7

the salvation of Israel?

When God reverses the captivity of his people

Jacob will rejoice and Israel will be glad.

\* (Psalm 53) A lament of an individual, duplicated in [Ps 14], except that “God” is used for “the L ORD,” and [Ps 53:6] is different, cf. [Ps 14].
