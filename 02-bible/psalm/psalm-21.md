# Psalm 21

## Thanksgiving and Assurances for the King

1 For the leader. A psalm of David. ^psalm-21-1

### I

2 LORD, the king finds joy in your power; ^psalm-21-2

in your victory how greatly he rejoices!

3 You have granted him his heart’s desire; ^psalm-21-3

you did not refuse the request of his lips.

*Selah*

4 For you welcomed him with goodly blessings; ^psalm-21-4

you placed on his head a crown of pure gold.

5 He asked life of you; ^psalm-21-5

you gave it to him,

length of days forever.

6 Great is his glory in your victory; ^psalm-21-6

majesty and splendor you confer upon him.

7 You make him the pattern of blessings forever, ^psalm-21-7

you gladden him with the joy of your face.

8 For the king trusts in the LORD, ^psalm-21-8

stands firm through the mercy of the Most High.

### II

9 Your hand will find all your enemies; ^psalm-21-9

your right hand will find your foes!

10 At the time of your coming ^psalm-21-10

you will make them a fiery furnace.

Then the LORD in his anger will consume them,

devour them with fire.

11 Even their descendants you will wipe out from the earth, ^psalm-21-11

their offspring from the human race.

12 Though they intend evil against you, ^psalm-21-12

devising plots, they will not succeed,

13 For you will put them to flight; ^psalm-21-13

you will aim at their faces with your bow.

### III

14 Arise, LORD, in your power! ^psalm-21-14

We will sing and chant the praise of your might.

\* (Psalm 21) The first part of this royal Psalm is a thanksgiving (([[psalm-21|Ps 21]]:2] – [8]), and the second is a promise that the king will triumph over his enemies (([[psalm-21|Ps 21]]:9] – [13]). The king’s confident prayer (([[psalm-21|Ps 21]]:3] – [5]) and trust in God (([[psalm-21|Ps 21]]:8]) enable him to receive the divine gifts of vitality, peace, and military success. ([[psalm-21|Ps 21]]:14] reprises ([[psalm-21|Ps 21]]:2]. When kings ceased in Israel after the sixth century B.C., the Psalm was sung of a future Davidic king.

a. (21:2) [Ps 63:12].

b. (21:5) [1 Kgs 3:14].

c. (21:14) [Nm 10:35].
