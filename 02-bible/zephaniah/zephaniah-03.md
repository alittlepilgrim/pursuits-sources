# Zephaniah, Chapter 3

## Chapter 3

### Iii. Jerusalem Reproached

1 Ah! Rebellious and polluted, ^zephaniah-03-1

the tyrannical city!

2 It listens to no voice, ^zephaniah-03-2

accepts no correction;

In the LORD it has not trusted,

nor drawn near to its God.

3 Its officials within it ^zephaniah-03-3

are roaring lions;

Its judges are desert wolves

that have no bones to gnaw by morning.

4 Its prophets are reckless, ^zephaniah-03-4

treacherous people;

Its priests profane what is holy,

and do violence to the law.

5 But the LORD in its midst is just, ^zephaniah-03-5

doing no wrong;

Morning after morning rendering judgment

unfailingly, at dawn;

the wicked, however, know no shame.

6 I have cut down nations, ^zephaniah-03-6

their battlements are laid waste;

I have made their streets deserted,

with no one passing through;

Their cities are devastated,

with no one dwelling in them.

7 I said, “Surely now you will fear me, ^zephaniah-03-7

you will accept correction;

They cannot fail to see

all I have brought upon them.”

Yet the more eagerly they have done

all their corrupt deeds.

### Iv. the Nations Punished and Jerusalem Restored

8 Therefore, wait for me—oracle of the LORD — ^zephaniah-03-8

until the day when I arise as accuser;

For it is my decision to gather nations,

to assemble kingdoms,

In order to pour out upon them my wrath,

all my blazing anger;

For in the fire of my passion

all the earth will be consumed.

9 For then I will make pure ^zephaniah-03-9

the speech of the peoples,

That they all may call upon the name of the LORD,

to serve him with one accord;

10 From beyond the rivers of Ethiopia ^zephaniah-03-10

and as far as the recesses of the North,

they shall bring me offerings.

11 On that day ^zephaniah-03-11

You will not be ashamed

of all your deeds,

when you rebelled against me;

For then I will remove from your midst

the proud braggarts,

And you shall no longer exalt yourself

on my holy mountain.

12 But I will leave as a remnant in your midst ^zephaniah-03-12

a people humble and lowly,

Who shall take refuge in the name of the LORD —

13 the remnant of Israel. ^zephaniah-03-13

They shall do no wrong

and speak no lies;

Nor shall there be found in their mouths

a deceitful tongue;

They shall pasture and lie down

with none to disturb them.

14 Shout for joy, daughter Zion! ^zephaniah-03-14

sing joyfully, Israel!

Be glad and exult with all your heart,

daughter Jerusalem!

15 The LORD has removed the judgment against you, ^zephaniah-03-15

he has turned away your enemies;

The King of Israel, the LORD, is in your midst,

you have no further misfortune to fear.

16 On that day, it shall be said to Jerusalem: ^zephaniah-03-16

Do not fear, Zion,

do not be discouraged!

17 The LORD, your God, is in your midst, ^zephaniah-03-17

a mighty savior,

Who will rejoice over you with gladness,

and renew you in his love,

Who will sing joyfully because of you,

18 as on festival days. ^zephaniah-03-18

I will remove disaster from among you,

so that no one may recount your disgrace.

19 At that time I will deal ^zephaniah-03-19

with all who oppress you;

I will save the lame,

and assemble the outcasts;

I will give them praise and renown

in every land where they were shamed.

20 At that time I will bring you home, ^zephaniah-03-20

and at that time I will gather you;

For I will give you renown and praise,

among all the peoples of the earth,

When I bring about your restoration

before your very eyes, says the LORD.
