# Article 1. at the Wellsprings of Prayer – In Brief

*2661 By a living transmission -Tradition - the Holy Spirit in the Church teaches the children of God to pray.* ^ccc-2661

*2662 The Word of God, the liturgy of the Church, and the virtues of faith, hope, and charity are sources of prayer.* ^ccc-2662
