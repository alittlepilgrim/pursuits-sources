# Article 1, the Sacrament of Baptism

1213 Holy [[baptism|Baptism]] is the basis of the whole Christian life, the gateway to life in the Spirit (vitae spiritualis ianua),<sup>4</sup> and the door which gives access to the other sacraments. Through Baptism we are freed from sin and reborn as sons of God; we become members of Christ, are incorporated into the Church and made sharers in her mission: "Baptism is the sacrament of regeneration through water in the word."<sup>5</sup> ^ccc-1213
