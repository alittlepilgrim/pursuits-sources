**CHAPTER ONE**

# Chapter One, I Believe in God the Father

**198** Our profession of faith begins with God, for God is the First and the Last,<sup>1</sup> The beginning and the end of everything. the Credo begins with God the Father, for the Father is the first divine person of the Most Holy Trinity; our Creed begins with the creation of heaven and earth, for creation is the beginning and the foundation of all God's works. ^ccc-198
