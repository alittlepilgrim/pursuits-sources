# Article 3, the Third Commandment

Remember the sabbath day, to keep it holy. Six days you shall labor, and do all your work; but the seventh day is a sabbath to the Lord your God; in it you shall not do any work.<sup>90</sup>

The sabbath was made for man, not man for the sabbath; so the Son of Man is lord even of the sabbath.<sup>91</sup>
