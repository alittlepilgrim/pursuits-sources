# 3.2.2.9. In Brief ^ccc-3

*2528 "Everyone who looks at a woman lustfully has already committed [[adultery]] with her in his heart" ([[matthew-05|Mt 5]]:28).* ^ccc-2528

*2529 The ninth commandment warns against lust or carnal concupiscence.* ^ccc-2529

*2530 The struggle against carnal lust involves purifying the heart and practicing temperance.* ^ccc-2530

*2531 Purity of heart will enable us to see God: it enables us even now to see things according to God.* ^ccc-2531

*2532 Purification of the heart demands prayer, the practice of chastity, purity of intention and of vision.* ^ccc-2532

*2533 Purity of heart requires the modesty which is patience, decency, and discretion. Modesty protects the intimate center of the person.* ^ccc-2533
