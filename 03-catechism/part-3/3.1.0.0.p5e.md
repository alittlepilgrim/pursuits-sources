# Section 1. Man's Vocation Life in the Spirit

1699 Life in the Holy Spirit fulfills the vocation of man (chapter one). This life is made up of divine charity and human solidarity (chapter two). It is graciously offered as salvation (chapter three). ^ccc-1699
