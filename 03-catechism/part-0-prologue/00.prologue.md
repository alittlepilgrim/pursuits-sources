# Prologue. Catechism of the Catholic Church

"FATHER,... this is eternal life, that they may know you, the only true God, and Jesus Christ whom you have sent." <sup>1</sup>

"God our Saviour desires all men to be saved and to come to the knowledge of the truth." <sup>2</sup>

"There is no other name under heaven given among men by which we must be saved" <sup>3</sup> - than the name of JESUS.
