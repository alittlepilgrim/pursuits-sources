# Gluttony

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Overindulgence in food or drink. Gluttony is one of the seven capital sins ([[3.1.1.8.p6d#^ccc-1866|1866]]).

## Ignatius Catholic Study Bible

**Gluttony:** Phil 3:19; Heb 12:16
