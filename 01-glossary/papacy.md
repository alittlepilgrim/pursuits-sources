# Papacy

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The supreme jurisdiction and ministry of the pope as shepherd of the whole Church. As successor of St. Peter, and therefore Bishop of Rome and Vicar of Christ, the pope is the perpetual and visible principle of unity in faith and communion in the Church ([[1.2.3.9.p2a#^ccc-882|882]]). _See_ Pope.
