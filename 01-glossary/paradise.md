# Paradise

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The symbolic description of the condition of our first parents before the Fall, who lived in a state of friendship with God in the happiness of original justice and holiness ([[1.2.1.1.6.p1b#^ccc-374|374]], [[1.2.1.1.6.p1b#^ccc-384|384]]). Paradise also signifies heaven, the state of those who live with Christ forever in the friendship and presence of God ([[1.2.3.12.p2m#^ccc-1023|1023]], [[3.1.1.2.p5k#^ccc-1721|1721]]).
