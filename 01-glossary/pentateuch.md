# Pentateuch

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The first five books of the Old Testament: Genesis, Exodus, Leviticus, Numbers, and Deuteronomy ([[1.2.3.8.p22#^ccc-702|702]]; cf. [[1.1.2.3.pr#^ccc-120|120]]).
