# Joseph (St.)

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- His obedience, spirit of faith, and humility 28,2; 373,1;
- his adhesion and abandonment to the Will of God and in the most obscure circumstances: 33,2; 373,2;
- together with Mary, — was the first to enter into that life of intimacy and love to which Jesus opened the door: 372,2;
- — is the model of souls of the interior life: 373,2;
- His mission of guardian of the Holy Family and Patron of the Church: 372,1;
- — has participated in full as a humble hidden collaborator in the work of Redemption: 372,1;
- — did not live for Himself, but solely for Jesus and Mary: 372,1; 373,2.
- 28. The Great Mystery Is Accomplished
- 33. Peace to Men
- 372. St. Joseph
- 373. St. Joseph, Life of Faith
