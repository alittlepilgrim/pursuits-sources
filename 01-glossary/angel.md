# Angel

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A spiritual, personal, and immortal creature, with intelligence and free will, who glorifies God without ceasing and who serves God as a messenger of his saving plan ([[1.2.1.1.5.p1a#^ccc-329|ccc-329]] - [[1.2.1.1.5.p1a#^ccc-331|ccc-331]]).
