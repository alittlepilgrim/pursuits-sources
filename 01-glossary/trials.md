# Trials

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- Spirit of faith in —: [[63-jesus-calms-the-tempest#Meditation 1|63,1]];
- in — the need to have recourse to God with confidence: [[63-jesus-calms-the-tempest#Meditation 2|63,2]];
- fidelity to God in —: [[105-the-transfiguration#Meditation 2|105,2]];
- humble acceptance of purifying —: [[237-infinite-justice#Meditation 2|237,2]]; [[351-passive-purification#Meditation 2|351,2]];
- if the soul is strong in accepting exterior —, God purifies by interior —: [[352-interior-trials#Meditation 1|352,1]];
- the greatest suffering of the soul in purifying — is to feel itself unworthy of the love of God and the fear that it has been rejected by Him: [[352-interior-trials#Meditation 1|352,1]];
- the fear of not being able to attain union with Him: [[353-desolation-and-darkness#Meditation 1|353,1]], [[353-desolation-and-darkness#Meditation 2|353,2]];
- the fear of not knowing how to love Him: [[355-the-development-of-love#Meditation 2|355,2]];
- every trial is a great mercy of God: [[237-infinite-justice#Meditation 2|237,2]];
- God tries the souls He loves: [[248-the-test-of-hope#Meditation 1|248,1]];
- God does not try us beyond our strength [[248-the-test-of-hope#Meditation 1|248,1]];
- by purifying — God helps the soul to increase in love: [[355-the-development-of-love#Meditation 1|355,1]];
- and trains the soul in the perfection of love: [[355-the-development-of-love#Meditation 2|355,2]].
