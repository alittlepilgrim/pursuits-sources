# Incarnation

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The fact that the Son of God assumed human nature and became man in order to accomplish our salvation in that same human nature. Jesus Christ, the Son of God, the second Person of the Trinity, is both true God and true man, not part God and part man ([[1.2.2.3.p1j#^ccc-461|461]], [[1.2.2.3.p1j#^ccc-464|464]]).
