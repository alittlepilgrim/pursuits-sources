# Passion of Jesus

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The suffering and death of Jesus ([[1.2.2.4.p1m#^ccc-572|572]], [[1.2.2.4.p1o#^ccc-602|602]]-[[1.2.2.4.p1o#^ccc-616|616]]). Passion or Palm Sunday begins Holy Week, during which the annual liturgical celebration of the Paschal Mystery of Christ takes place ([[1.2.2.3.p1l#^ccc-560|560]]).
