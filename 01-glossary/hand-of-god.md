# Hand of God

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- God alone can satisfy us: 141,1; 143,1; 234,1
- Hunger and thirst for God: 141, 4; 234,1; 358,1, 358,2
- Our life is one continual seeking of God: 141,2; 379,2
- The seeking of God is the preoccupation of the loving soul: 142,1, 142,2
- Transcendence of God: 142,2; 227,1; 254,1
- God infinitely surpasses our understanding: 191,1; 196,2; 227,1
- Perfect orientation toward God impossible without the help of the Holy Spirit: 191,1; 195,1
- Infinite distance between us and God: 353,1
- While on earth we know God through the mirror of creatures: 191,1; 227,2
- Perfections of creatures are nothing in comparison with those of God: 191,1; 227,2; 228,1, 228,2
- Our contact with God should be that of children: 198,1; 245,1; 282,1, 282,2
- We should consider God as a Father: 198,1; 245,1; 282,1, 282,2; 299,1; 304,1
- God is our Father in spite of our miseries: 350,1
- God is infinite Being eternally subsisting: 228,1
- God possesses all perfections without defects or limits: 228,2; 230,1
- God is ONE in His myriad perfections: 228,2
- God is Being absolutely simple to the exclusion of all duplicity and multiplicity: 229,1, 229,2
- God wishes good always and solely: 229,2; 230,1; 232,1
- God is immutable; His thought and will do not change: 230,1
- God is eternal; He possesses the fullness of His infinite life totally at the same time: 230,2
- God is infinite Wisdom who knows all things in the most perfect manner: 234,1
- God is infinite Goodness who wishes only good and who draws good even out of evil even from sin: 232,1; 244,1, 244,2
- All that comes from the Hand of God bears the impress of His goodness: 232,2
- This infinite goodness immeasurably surpasses all human malice: 232,2
- Goodness of God gratuitous and inexhaustible: 233,2
- God is infinite Love; all that is in Him is love: 235,1
- The love of God is the Cause that infuses and creates goodness in things: 235,1
- All that we have; all that we are is the gift of His infinite love: 235,1
- God goes before us and continually accompanies us with His love: 235,1
- God loves us when He consoles us and also when He afflicts us with sorrow: 235,1
- God loves us not because we are without sin but because we are His children: 249,2
- God has loved us to the extent of becoming like to us so that it is possible to admit us within the circle of the divine friendship: 251,1
- And to unite us to Himself by love: 348,2; 358,1
- God has first loved us and made us capable of returning His love: 251,2
- The love of God for us is merciful love: 236,1
- God is merciful because He is just and He is just because He is merciful: 237,1
- In His mercy, God gives as much as He can in accordance with justice: 237,1
- God does not demand more than one can give Him: 329,2
- God is Providence that ordains and directs all for our good: 239,1; 244,2
- God permits suffering only for our good: 239,1; 248,1; 292,1
- God has special care for each creature even the least: 239,2
- God governs all things with infinite sweetness: 270,1
- Nothing can obstruct His omnipotence: 240,1, 240,2
- Omnipotence of God is totally at the service of His infinite goodness: 240,1
- God is helpful omnipotence always ready to come to our assistance: 246,2
