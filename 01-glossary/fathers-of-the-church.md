# Fathers of the Church

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Church teachers and writers of the early centuries whose teachings are a witness to the Tradition of the Church ([[1.1.2.2.pk#^ccc-78|78]], [[1.2.3.8.p1z#^ccc-688|688]]).
