# Sanctuary

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The part of a church set apart for the principal rites of worship (cf. [[2.1.2.1.p3a#^ccc-1183|1183]]). (2) A shrine or place of pilgrimage ([[2.2.4.1.p58#^ccc-1674|1674]]).
