# Apparition

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

An appearance to people on earth of a heavenly being--Christ, Mary, an angel, or a saint. The apparitions of Jesus in his risen body to his disciples occurred between Easter and his Ascension into heaven ([[1.2.2.5.p1s|ccc-641]], [[1.2.2.6.p1t|ccc-659]]).
