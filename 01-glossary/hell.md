# Hell

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The state of definitive self-exclusion from communion with God and the blessed, reserved for those who refuse by their own free choice to believe and be converted from sin, even to the end of their lives ([[1.2.3.12.p2o#^ccc-1033|1033]]).
