# Communion

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Holy Communion, the reception of the Body and Blood of Christ in the Eucharist ([[2.2.1.3.p42#^ccc-1382|1382]]). More generally, our fellowship and union with Jesus and other baptized Christians in the Church, which has its source and summit in the celebration of the Eucharist. In this sense, Church as communion is the deepest vocation of the Church ([[1.2.3.9.p2b#^ccc-959|959]]).
