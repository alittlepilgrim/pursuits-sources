# Magisterium

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The living, teaching office of the Church, whose task it is to give as authentic interpretation of the word of God, whether in its written form (Sacred Scripture), or in the form of Tradition. The Magisterium ensures the Church's fidelity to the teaching of the Apostles in matters of faith and morals ([[1.1.2.2.pm|ccc-85]], [[1.2.3.9.p2a|ccc-890]], [[3.1.3.3.p74|ccc-2033]]).
