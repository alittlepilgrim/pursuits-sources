# Commandment

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A norm of moral and/or religious action; above all, the _Ten Commandments_ given by God to Moses. Jesus summarized all the commandments in the twofold command of love of God and love of neighbor ([[3.2.0.0.p78#^ccc-2052|2052]]).
