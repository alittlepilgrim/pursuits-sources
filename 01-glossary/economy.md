# Economy

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The structure and organization of productive work or activity in a society, forming the basis for financial support and stability of individuals, families, and society. The morality of economic activity is judged according to the seventh commandment; economic activity is one of the principal points addressed by the Church's social doctrine ([[3.2.2.7.p8d#^ccc-2426|2426]], [[3.2.2.7.p8d#^ccc-2430|2430]]).
