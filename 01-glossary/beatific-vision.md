# Beatific Vision

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The contemplation of God in heavenly glory, a gift of God which is a constitutive element of the happiness (or beatitude) of heaven ([[1.2.3.12.p2m|ccc-1028]], [[3.1.1.2.p5k|ccc-1720]]).
