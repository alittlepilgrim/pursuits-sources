# Divine Office

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The Liturgy of the Hours, the public prayer of the Church which sanctifies the whole course of the day and night. Christ thus continues his priestly work through the prayer of his priestly people ([[2.1.2.1.p39#^984616|ccc-1174]]).
