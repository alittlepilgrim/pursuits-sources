# Word of God

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The entire content of Revelation as contained in the Holy Bible and proclaimed in the Church. In John's Gospel, God's "Word" means his only-begotten Son, who is the fullness of God's Revelation and who took flesh (the Word incarnate) and became man for the sake of our salvation ([[1.1.2.1.ph#^ccc-65|65]], [[1.1.2.2.pl#^ccc-81|81]], [[1.1.2.3.po#^ccc-101|101]], [[1.2.1.1.p17#^ccc-241|241]], [[1.2.2.3.p1j#^ccc-461|461]]; cf. [[4.1.2.1.p9d#^ccc-2653|2653]]).
