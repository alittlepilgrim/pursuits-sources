# Impediment

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

An obstacle that makes a person ineligible for performing an act or receiving a sacrament, e.g., [[holy-orders|Holy Orders]] or Matrimony (cf. [[2.2.3.7.p53#^ccc-1635|1635]]).
