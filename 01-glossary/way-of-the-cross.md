# Way of the Cross

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A devotional exercise which follows the "way of the cross" in the Savior's steps, observing stops or "stations" to meditate on the path Jesus took from the Praetorium in Jerusalem to Golgotha and the tomb. "By his holy Cross he has redeemed the world" ([[4.1.2.2.p9f#^ccc-2669|2669]]).
