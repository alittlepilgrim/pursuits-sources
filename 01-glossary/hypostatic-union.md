# Hypostatic Union

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The union of the divine and human natures in the one divine Person (Greek: _hypostasis_) of the Son of God, Jesus Christ ([[1.2.1.1.p17#^ccc-252|252]], [[1.2.2.3.p1j#^ccc-468|468]]). ### -I-
