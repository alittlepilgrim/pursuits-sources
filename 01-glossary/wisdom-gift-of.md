# Wisdom (Gift Of)

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- makes one relish the things of God [[313-the-gift-of-wisdom#Meditation 1|313,1]];
- — actuates us by charity: [[313-the-gift-of-wisdom#Meditation 2|313,2]];
- introduces the soul to the prayer of union: [[313-the-gift-of-wisdom#Meditation 2|313,2]];
- under its influence the soul judges all things according to the divine judgment: [[314-blessed-are-the-peacemakers#Meditation 1|314,1]];
- to — corresponds the beatitude of the peacemakers: [[314-blessed-are-the-peacemakers#Meditation 1|314,1]];
- — establishes the soul in the peace of the children of God: [[314-blessed-are-the-peacemakers#Meditation 2|314,2]].
