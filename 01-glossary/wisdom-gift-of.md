# Wisdom (Gift Of)

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- makes one relish the things of God 313,1;
- — actuates us by charity: 313,2;
- introduces the soul to the prayer of union: 313,2;
- under its influence the soul judges all things according to the divine judgment: 314,1;
- to — corresponds the beatitude of the peacemakers: 314,1;
- — establishes the soul in the peace of the children of God: 314,2.
