# Abraham

The man of faith and patriarch of Israel with whom God made a covenant which promised him land in which to live and many descendants, a great people for whom the Lord would be their God. Through [[abraham|Abraham]] God formed the people to whom he would later give the law by revelation to Moses. With the advent of Christ, the people of Israel would serve as the root to which the Gentiles would be grafted by their coming to believe ([[1.1.2.1.pg|ccc-59]], [[1.1.2.1.pi|ccc-72]], [[1.1.3.1.pv|ccc-145]], [[1.2.3.8.p22|ccc-705]], [[1.2.3.9.p27|ccc-762]], [[4.1.1.1.p91|ccc-2570]]).
