# Punishment, Temporal

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Purification of the unhealthy attachment to creatures, which is a consequence of sin that perdures even after death. We must be purified either during our earthly life through prayer and a conversion which comes from fervent charity, or after death in purgatory ([[2.2.2.4.p4g#^ccc-1472|1472]]).
