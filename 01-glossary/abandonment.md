## The Way of Perfection

ABANDONMENT (TO GOD'S WILL). A free surrender of one's will to God (wop-32.4). Christ is the model of abandonment; in the garden he manifested his own desire and fear, but then abandoned them to God (30.2). Great gain results from total abandonment to God's will, especially in fear of death and sickness (11.4; 32.4).
