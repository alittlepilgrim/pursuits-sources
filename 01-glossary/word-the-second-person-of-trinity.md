# Word (The) Second Person of the Most Blessed Trinity

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- In every way is equal to the Father and the Holy Spirit: 27,1;
- Incarnate for love of us: 27,2;
- in order to communicate to us supernatural being: 39,2;
- becoming Incarnate — remains as He was 27,2; 52,1;
- becoming Incarnate — conceals His greatness and His Divinity: 31,1.
- See: Jesus, [[incarnation|Incarnation]].
