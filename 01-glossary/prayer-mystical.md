# Mystical Prayer[^1]

This is the prayer found in the last four dwelling places. It is called by Teresa “supernatural prayer” because it is a gift of God and cannot be acquired through our own efforts.

There are different kinds of [[prayer|Prayer]] and for Teresa [of Avila] they are as follows: [[prayer-ascetical|Ascetical Prayer]], [[prayer-painful|Painful Prayer]] and Mystical Prayer.
