# Mystagogy

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A liturgical catechesis which aims to initiate people into the mystery of Christ. In a more specific sense, the catechetical period following immediately after the reception of Baptism by adults ([[2.0.0.0.p2t#^ccc-1075|1075]]). ### -N-
