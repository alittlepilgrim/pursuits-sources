# Evangelization

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The proclamation of Christ and his Gospel (Greek: _evangelion_) by word and the testimony of life, in fulfillment of Christ's command ([[1.2.3.9.p2a#^ccc-905|905]]; cf. [[1.2.3.9.p29#^ccc-861|861]]).

## Ignatius Catholic Study Bible

**Evangelization**

Apologetics, or being ready to give a reasoned account for one's hope in Christ: 1 Pet 3:15

Jesus' commissioning of his disciples to evangelize the whole world: Mt 28:19-20; Mk 16:15; Lk 24:47-48; Acts 1:8

Mission of evangelization directed to Jew and Gentile alike: Mt 24:14; Mk 13:10; Acts 13:47; 14:15; 17:30-31; Rom 2:10; 10:12-18; 11:12, 15, 25-26; 1 Cor 1:24; 12:13
