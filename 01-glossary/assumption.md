# Assumption

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The dogma which recognizes the Blessed Virgin Mary's singular participation in her Son's Resurrection by which she was taken up body and soul into heavenly glory, when the course of her earthly life was finished ([[1.2.3.9.p2c#^ccc-966|966]]).
