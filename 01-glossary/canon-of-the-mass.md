# Canon of the Mass

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The central part of the Mass, also known as the Eucharistic Prayer or "[[anaphora]]," which contains the prayer of thanksgiving and consecration ([[2.2.1.3.p40#^ccc-1352|1352]]).
