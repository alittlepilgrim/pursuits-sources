# Hermit

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

One who lives the eremitical life. Through silence and solitude, in prayer and penance, the hermit or anchorite vows, although not necessarily publicly, to follow the evangelical counsels out of love for God and desire for the salvation of the world ([[1.2.3.9.p2a#^ccc-920|920]]).
