# Love of Self

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- Heart occupied by — cannot be filled with God: [[22-behold-the-savior-comes#Meditation 2|22,2]];
- impossible to serve God and — at the same time: [[117-true-glory#Meditation 2|117,2]];

- struggle against demands of self-love [[117-true-glory#Meditation 1|117,1]], [[117-true-glory#Meditation 2|117,2]];

- cause of anger in face of opposition: [[214-the-heart-of-jesus-our-model#Meditation 2|214,2]];
