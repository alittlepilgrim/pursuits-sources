# Liturgy

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

In its original meaning, a "public work" or service done in the name of or on behalf of the people. Through the liturgy Christ our High Priest continues the work of our redemption through the Church's celebration of the Paschal Mystery by which he accomplished our salvation ([[2.0.0.0.p2t#^ccc-1067|1067]]-[[2.0.0.0.p2t#^ccc-1069|1069]]).
