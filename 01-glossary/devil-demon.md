# Devil / Demon

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A fallen angel, who sinned against God by refusing to accept his reign. Satan or the devil, the Evil One, and the other demons were at first good angels, created naturally good, who became evil by their own doing ([[1.2.1.1.p1c#^ccc-391|391]], [[3.1.1.1.p5g#^ccc-1707|1707]]; cf. [[4.2.0.3.pac#^ccc-2851|2851]]).
