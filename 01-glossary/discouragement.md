# Discouragement

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- Due to pride and lack of confidence in God: 109,1; 249,2; 295,1;
- makes one recede in the path of perfection: 295,1;
- not to be discouraged by falls, but to humble oneself and begin again: 193,2; 293,2; 294,1; 295,1;
- not to be discouraged by one’s misery and impotence: 247,2; 252,1;
- in temptations to — to react with an act of confidence in God: 248,2.
