# Attachments

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

Every voluntary attachment hinders union with God: 79,1;

the least attachment to sin and imperfection hinders union with God: 237,2;

habitual — are bonds that bind one to earth: 79,2;

attachment to riches is obstacle to salvation 84,2; 85,1, 85,2;

the root of every attachment is the disordered tendency to pleasure: 81,1; 82,1.
