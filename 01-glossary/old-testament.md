# Old Testament

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The forty-six books of the Bible, which record the history of salvation from creation through the old alliance or covenant with Israel, in preparation for the appearance of Christ as Savior of the world ([[1.1.2.3.pr#^ccc-120|120]]-[[1.1.2.3.pr#^ccc-121|121]]). _See_ Bible; Covenant.
