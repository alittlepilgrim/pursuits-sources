# Doctrine / Dogma

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The revealed teachings of Christ which are proclaimed by the fullest extent of the exercise of the authority of the Church's Magisterium. The faithful are obliged to believe the truths or dogmas contained in divine Revelation and defined by the Magisterium ([[1.1.2.2.pm#^ccc-88|88]]).
