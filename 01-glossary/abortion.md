# Abortion

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Deliberate termination of pregnancy by killing the unborn child. Such direct [[abortion]], willed either as an end or a means, is gravely contrary to the moral law. The Church attaches the canonical penalty of excommunication to this crime against human life ([[3.2.2.5.p7z|2271-2272]]).
