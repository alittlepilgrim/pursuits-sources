# Eastern Churches

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Churches of the East in union with Rome (the Western Church), but not of Roman rite, with their own liturgical, theological, and administrative traditions, such as those of the Byzantine, Alexandrian or Coptic, Syriac, Armenian, Maronite, and Chaldean rites. The variety of particular churches with dis- tinctive traditions witnesses to the catholicity of the one Church of Christ, which takes root in distinct cultures ([[2.1.2.2.p3c#^ccc-1202|1202]]-[[2.1.2.2.p3c#^ccc-1203|1203]]; cf. [[1.2.3.9.p29#^ccc-835|835]]).
