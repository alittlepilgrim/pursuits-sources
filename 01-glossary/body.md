# Body

## The Way of Perfection

BODY. The external part of the human composite (body and soul) ([[tj-wop-chapter-31#^wop-ch31-2|31.2]]–[[tj-wop-chapter-31#^wop-ch31-3|3]]). Its senses or powers are mainly those of sight and hearing ([[tj-wop-chapter-28#^wop-ch28-5|28.5]]–[[tj-wop-chapter-28#^wop-ch28-6|6]]; [[tj-wop-chapter-31#^wop-ch31-2|31.2]]; [[tj-wop-chapter-34#^wop-ch34-12|34.12]]), which cannot on their own perceive the Lord, truly present in the Blessed Sacrament ([[tj-wop-chapter-34#^wop-ch34-5|34.5]]), or when he speaks to the heart ([[tj-wop-chapter-24#^wop-ch24-5|24.5]]). The more comforts the body receives, the more needs it discovers ([[tj-wop-chapter-11#^wop-ch11-2|11.2]]); its senses can be a source of distraction ([[tj-wop-chapter-28#^wop-ch28-5|28.5]]); thus it can become an enemy in the spiritual battle of this life ([[tj-wop-chapter-11#^wop-ch11-5|11.5]]). The body should be put to work for one's daily sustenance ([[tj-wop-chapter-34#^wop-ch34-4|34.4]]). In sickness it should be cared for, and compassion should be shown toward the sick ([[tj-wop-chapter-11#^wop-ch11-1|11.1]]). The devil tempts people toward bodily penances that can ruin their health ([[tj-wop-chapter-19#^wop-ch19-9|19.9]]–[[tj-wop-chapter-19#^wop-ch19-10|10]]). In the prayer of quiet the body experiences rest and delight ([[tj-wop-chapter-31#^wop-ch31-2|31.2]]–[[tj-wop-chapter-31#^wop-ch31-3|3]]). In rapture, awareness through the exterior senses is lost ([[tj-wop-chapter-32#^wop-ch32-12|32.12]]).
