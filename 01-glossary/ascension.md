# Ascension

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The entry of Jesus' humanity into divine glory in God's heavenly domain, forty days after his Resurrection ([[1.2.2.6.p1t|ccc-659]], [[1.2.2.6.p1u|ccc-665]]).
