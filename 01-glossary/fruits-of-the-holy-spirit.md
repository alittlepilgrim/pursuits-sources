# Fruits of the Holy Spirit

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The perfections that the Holy Spirit forms in us as the "first fruits" of eternal glory. The tradition of the Church identifies twelve fruits of the Holy Spirit ([[3.1.1.7.p67#^ccc-1832|1832]]). ### -G-
