# Examination of Conscience

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Prayerful self-reflection on our words and deeds in the light of the Gospel to determine how we may have sinned against God. The reception of the Sacrament of Penance ought to be prepared for by such an examination of conscience ([[2.2.2.4.p4d#^ccc-1454|1454]]).
