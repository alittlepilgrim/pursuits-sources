# Son of God

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A title frequently applied to Jesus in the Gospel, signifying his unique relationship to the Father. The second Person of the Blessed Trinity is called Son of God in reference to the Eternal Father. The revelation of his divine sonship is the principal dramatic development of the story of Jesus of Nazareth ([[1.2.2.2.p1g#^ccc-441|441]]-[[1.2.2.2.p1g#^ccc-445|445]]).
