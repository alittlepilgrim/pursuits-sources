# Sacrilege

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Profanation of or irreverence toward persons, places, and things which are sacred, i.e., dedicated to God; sacrilege against the sacraments, especially the Eucharist, is a particularly grave offense against the first commandment ([[3.2.1.1.p7e#^ccc-2120|2120]]).
