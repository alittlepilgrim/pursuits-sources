# Haughtiness

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- Has the sad power of impeding the work of the infinite mercy of God: [[236-infinite-mercy#Meditation 1|236,1]]; [[259-charity-and-humility#Meditation 2|259,2]];

- hinders charity: [[259-charity-and-humility#Meditation 2|259,2]];
- he who is haughty is a seeding ground of discord: [[294-jesus-our-life#Meditation 1|294,1]];
- — is an obstacle to faith: [[315-the-power-and-love-of-jesus#Meditation 1|315,1]].
