# Initiation, Christian

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The foundations of every Christian life laid by the Sacraments of Baptism, Confirmation, and Eucharist. The process by which a non-baptized person is prepared to become a full member of the Church is called the catechumenate, which was restored in the Latin Church by the Second Vatican Council, and whose distinct stages and rites are found in the _Rite of Christian Initiation of Adults_ ([[2.2.1.0.p3f#^ccc-1212|1212]], [[2.2.1.0.p3j#^ccc-1230|1230]]).
