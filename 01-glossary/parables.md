# Parables

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A characteristic feature of the teaching of Jesus. Parables are simple images or comparisons which confront the hearer or reader with a radical choice about his invitation to enter the Kingdom of God ([[1.2.2.3.p1l#^ccc-546|546]]).
