# Patristic

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Pertaining to the writings of the holy Fathers of the Church, who are privileged witnesses of the apostolic tradition ([[1.1.2.2.pk#^ccc-78|78]], [[1.2.3.8.p1z#^ccc-688|688]]). _See_Fathers of the Church.
