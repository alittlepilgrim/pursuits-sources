# Sacramentals

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Sacred signs which bear a certain resemblance to the sacraments, and by means of which spiritual effects are signified and obtained through the prayers of the Church ([[2.2.4.1.p58#^ccc-1667|1667]]).
