# Iconoclasm

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A heresy which maintained that veneration of religious images is unlawful. Iconoclasm was condemned as unfaithful to Christian tradition at the Second Ecumenical Council of Nicaea in 787 A.D. ([[3.2.1.1.p7f#^ccc-2131|2131]]).
