# Eternal Life

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Living forever with God in the happiness of heaven, entered after death by the souls of those who die in the grace and friendship of God ([[1.2.3.11.p2g#^ccc-988|988]], [[1.2.3.12.p2k#^ccc-1020|1020]]). In preaching the kingdom of heaven, Jesus called all people to eternal life, which is anticipated in the grace of union with Christ: "This is eternal life, that they may know you, the only true God, and Jesus Christ whom you have sent" (_Jn_17:3).
