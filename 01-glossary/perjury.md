# Perjury

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Giving one's word under oath falsely, or making a promise under oath without intending to keep it. Perjury violates the second and eighth commandments ([[3.2.1.2.p7j#^ccc-2152|2152]], [[3.2.2.8.p8k#^ccc-2476|2476]]).
