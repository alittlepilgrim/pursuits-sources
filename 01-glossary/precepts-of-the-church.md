# Precepts of the Church

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Positive laws (sometimes called commandments) made by Church authorities to guarantee for the faithful the indispensable minimum in prayer and moral effort, for the sake of their growth in love of God and neighbor ([[3.1.3.3.p75#^ccc-2041|2041]]).
