# Atheism

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The denial in theory and/or practice that God exists. Atheism is a sin against the virtue of religion required by the first commandment of the law ([[3.2.1.1.p7e|ccc-2124]]-[[3.2.1.1.p7e|ccc-2125]]).
