# Virtue

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

An habitual and firm disposition to do the good. The moral virtues are acquired through human effort aided by God's grace; the theological virtues are gifts of God ([[3.1.1.7.p64#^ccc-1803|1803]]). _See_ Cardinal Virtues.
