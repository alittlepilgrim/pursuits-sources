# Christian

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A name derived from that of Christ himself. The name refers to all those who have been anointed through the gift of the Holy Spirit in Baptism; hence, the followers of Christ, the members of the Christian Church. According to [[acts-of-the-apostles-11|Acts 11]]:26 "it was in Antioch that the disciples were first called Christians" ([[2.2.1.2.p3q|ccc-1289]]).
