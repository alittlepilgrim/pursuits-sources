# Deposit of Faith

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The heritage of faith contained in Sacred Scripture and Tradition, handed on in the Church from the time of the Apostles, from which the Magisterium draws all that it proposes for belief as being divinely revealed ([[1.1.2.2.pm#^ccc-84|84]]; cf. [[2.1.2.2.p3c#^ccc-1202|1202]]).
