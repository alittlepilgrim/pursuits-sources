# Prophet

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

One sent by God to form the people of the Old Covenant in the hope of salvation. The prophets are often authors of books of the Old Testament ([[1.2.3.8.p22#^ccc-702|702]]). The prophetic books constitute a major section of the Old Testament of the Bible ([[1.1.2.1.pg#^ccc-64|64]], [[1.1.2.3.pr#^ccc-120|120]], [[1.2.2.3.p1l#^ccc-522|522]], [[4.1.1.1.p91#^ccc-2581|2581]]). John the Baptist concludes the work of the prophets of the Old Covenant ([[1.2.3.8.p23#^ccc-721|721]]).
