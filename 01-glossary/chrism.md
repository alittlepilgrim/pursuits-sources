# Chrism

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Perfumed oil, consecrated by the bishop, which signifies the gift of the Holy Spirit. Chrism is used for consecration in the Sacraments of Baptism, Confirmation, and [[holy-orders|Holy Orders]] ([[2.2.1.0.p3j#^ccc-1241|1241]], [[2.2.1.2.p3q#^ccc-1289|1289]], [[2.2.1.2.p3q#^ccc-1291|1291]], [[2.2.1.2.p3r#^ccc-1294|1294]]).
