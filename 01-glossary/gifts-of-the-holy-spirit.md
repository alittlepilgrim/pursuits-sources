# Gifts of the Holy Spirit

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Permanent dispositions that make us docile to follow the promptings of the Holy Spirit. The traditional list of seven gifts of the Spirit is derived from Isaiah 11:1-3: wisdom, understanding, knowledge, counsel, piety, fortitude, and fear of the Lord ([[3.1.1.7.p67#^ccc-1830|1830]]).
