# Immaculate Conception

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The dogma proclaimed in Christian Tradition and defined in 1854, that from the first moment of her conception, Mary--by the singular grace of God and by virtue of the merits of Jesus Christ--was preserved immune from original sin ([[1.2.2.3.p1k#^ccc-491|491]]).
