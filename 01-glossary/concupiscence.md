# Concupiscence

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Human appetites or desires which remain disordered due to the temporal consequences of original sin, which remain even after Baptism, and which produce an inclination to sin ([[2.2.1.0.p3n#^ccc-1264|1264]], [[2.2.2.4.p48#^ccc-1426|1426]], [[3.2.2.9.p8p#^ccc-2515|2515]]).
