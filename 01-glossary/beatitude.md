# Beatitude

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Happiness or blessedness, especially the eternal happiness of heaven, which is described as the vision of God, or entering into God's rest by those whom He makes "partakers of the divine nature" ([[1.2.3.12.p2m|ccc-1024]], [[3.1.1.2.p5k|ccc-1721]]).
