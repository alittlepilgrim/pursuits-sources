# Filioque

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A word meaning "and (from) the Son," added to the Latin version of the Niceno-Constantinopolitan Creed, by which the Latin tradition of the Creed confesses that the Holy Spirit "proceeds from the Father _and the Son_" ([[1.2.1.1.p17#^ccc-246|246]]).
