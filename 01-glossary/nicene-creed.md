# Nicene Creed

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The profession of faith, common to the churches of East and West, which came from the first two ecumenical councils (Nicaea and Constantinople: [[1.2.1.1.5.p1a#^ccc-325|325]] and [[1.2.1.1.6.p1b#^ccc-381|381]] a.d.) ([[1.2.0.0.p14#^ccc-195|195]]-[[1.2.0.0.p14#^ccc-196|196]]). _See_ Creed.
