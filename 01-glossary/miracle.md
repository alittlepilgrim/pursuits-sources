# Miracle

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A sign or wonder, such as a healing or the control of nature, which can only be attributed to divine power. The miracles of Jesus were messianic signs of the presence of God's kingdom ([[1.2.2.3.p1l#^ccc-547|547]]).
