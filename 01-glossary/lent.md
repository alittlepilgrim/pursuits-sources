# Lent

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The liturgical season of forty days which begins with Ash Wednesday and ends with the celebration of the Paschal Mystery (Easter Triduum). Lent is the primary penitential season in the Church's liturgical year, reflecting the forty days Jesus spent in the desert in fasting and prayer ([[1.2.2.3.p1l#^ccc-540|540]], [[2.1.1.1.p2x#^ccc-1095|1095]], [[2.2.2.4.p4b#^ccc-1438|1438]]).
