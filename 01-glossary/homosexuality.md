# Homosexuality

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Sexual attraction or orientation toward persons of the same sex and/or sexual acts between persons of the same sex. Homosexual acts are morally wrong because they violate God's purpose for human sexual activity ([[3.2.2.6.p85#^ccc-2357|2357]]).
