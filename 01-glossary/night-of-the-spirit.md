# Night of the Spirit

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- Uproots imperfect habits: [[347-the-night-of-the-spirit#Meditation 1|347,1]];
- achieves in the soul a deep and radical detachment: [[349-toward-complete-purification#Meditation 1|349,1]];
- to enter the — there is need of renunciation of self, not only in regard to material goods, but also in regard to moral and spiritual goods [[347-the-night-of-the-spirit#Meditation 2|347,2]];
- need to exercise oneself intensely in the theological virtues: [[349-toward-complete-purification#Meditation 2|349,2]];
- anguish and darkness the soul suffers in this —: [[353-desolation-and-darkness#Meditation 1|353,1]], [[353-desolation-and-darkness#Meditation 2|353,2]];
- its sole support is pure and naked faith: [[353-desolation-and-darkness#Meditation 2|353,2]];
- one of the greatest graces God can grant a soul is to introduce it into the —: [[348-purifying-love#Meditation 1|348,1]];
- this —, though very bitter, is the work of Divine Love: [[348-purifying-love#Meditation 1|348,1]];
- only God can lead the soul into the depth of this —: [[351-passive-purification#Meditation 1|351,1]];
- the — culminates in interior pain in which God annihilates it in its spiritual faculties and destroys in it every imperfect habit: [[352-interior-trials#Meditation 1|352,1]];
- in the — the soul is reduced to the center of its nothingness: [[352-interior-trials#Meditation 2|352,2]];
- in this — the soul is in a special way under the influence of the Holy Spirit: [[354-confidence-and-abandonment#Meditation 2|354,2]];
- the obscure night of the apostolic life: [[338-apostolic-hope#Meditation 2|338,2]];
- while we are on earth there will always be something of the —: [[354-confidence-and-abandonment#Meditation 1|354,1]].
