# Punishment, Eternal

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The penalty for unrepented mortal sin, separating the sinner from communion with God for all eternity; the condemnation of the unrepentant sinner to hell ([[1.2.3.12.p2o#^ccc-1035|1035]]).
