# Resurrection of Christ

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The bodily rising of Jesus from the dead on the third day after his death on the cross and burial in the tomb. The resurrection of Christ is the crowning truth of our faith in Christ ([[1.2.2.5.p1s#^ccc-638|638]]).
