# Catholic Church

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The Church established by Christ on the foundation of the Apostles, possessing the fullness of the means of salvation which he has willed: correct and complete confession of faith, full sacramental life, and ordained ministry in apostolic succession ([[1.2.3.9.p29#^ccc-830|830]]).
