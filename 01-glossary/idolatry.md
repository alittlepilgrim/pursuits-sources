# Idolatry

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The divinization of a creature in place of God; the substitution of some one (or thing) for God; worshiping a creature (even money, pleasure, or power) instead of the Creator ([[3.2.1.1.p7e#^ccc-2112|2112]]).
