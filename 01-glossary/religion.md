# Religion

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A set of beliefs and practices followed by those committed to the service and worship of God. The first commandment requires us to believe in God, to worship and serve him, as the first duty of the virtue of religion ([[3.2.1.1.p7c#^ccc-2084|2084]], [[3.2.1.1.p7g#^ccc-2135|2135]]).
