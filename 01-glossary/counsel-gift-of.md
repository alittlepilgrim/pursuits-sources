# Counsel

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

_See_ Evangelical Counsels; Gifts of the Holy Spirit.

# Counsel (Gift Of)

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- To aid prudence: [[306-the-gift-of-counsel#Meditation 1|306,1]];
- by means of — the soul perceives the voice of the Holy Spirit: [[306-the-gift-of-counsel#Meditation 1|306,1]];
- by means of this gift the Holy Spirit wishes to be our Counselor: [[306-the-gift-of-counsel#Meditation 2|306,2]];
- recollection and docility necessary to profit by this gift: [[306-the-gift-of-counsel#Meditation 2|306,2]];
- its special effect is mercy: [[307-blessed-are-the-merciful#Meditation 1|307,1]];
- it corresponds to the beatitude of mercy: [[307-blessed-are-the-merciful#Meditation 1|307,1]], [[307-blessed-are-the-merciful#Meditation 2|307,2]];
- it enlightens us on our own misery: [[307-blessed-are-the-merciful#Meditation 2|307,2]].
