# Counsel

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

_See_ Evangelical Counsels; Gifts of the Holy Spirit.

# Counsel (Gift Of)

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- To aid prudence: 306,1;
- by means of — the soul perceives the voice of the Holy Spirit: 306,1;
- by means of this gift the Holy Spirit wishes to be our Counselor: 306,2;
- recollection and docility necessary to profit by this gift: 306,2;
- its special effect is mercy: 307,1;
- it corresponds to the beatitude of mercy: 307,1, 307,2;
- it enlightens us on our own misery: 307,2.
- 306. The Gift of Counsel
- 307. Blessed Are the Merciful
