# Superstition

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The attribution of a kind of magical power to certain practices or objects, like charms or omens. Reliance on such power, rather than on trust in God, constitutes an offense against the honor due to God alone, as required by the first commandment ([[3.2.1.1.p7e#^ccc-2110|2110]]).
