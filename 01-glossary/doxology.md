# Doxology

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Christian prayer which gives praise and glory to God, often in a special way to the three divine persons of the Trinity. Liturgical prayers traditionally conclude with the doxology "to the Father, through the Son, in the Holy Spirit"; the final doxology of the Lord's Prayer renews the prayer's first three petitions in the form of [[adoration]] and praise ([[4.1.1.3.p9a#^ccc-2639|2639]], [[4.2.0.4.pad#^ccc-2855|2855]]). ### -E-
