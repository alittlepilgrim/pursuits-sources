# Renunciation

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- of self is indispensable foundation of spiritual life: [[54-what-jesus-teaching-exacts#Meditation 2|54,2]];
- total — necessary in order to attain union with God: [[79-voluntary-attachments#Meditation 2|79,2]]; [[80-the-essence-of-detachment#Meditation 1|80,1]];
- when the soul has attained total — of self, it. will acquire the precious pearl of divine union: [[82-rules-for-detachment#Meditation 1|82,1]];
- — places one on the path of conformity to Jesus Crucified: [[194-the-way-of-the-cross#Meditation 2|194,2]];
- — frees the soul so that it can use all its strength to love God: [[253-the-precept-of-charity#Meditation 2|253,2]];
- — is an exercise of love: [[253-the-precept-of-charity#Meditation 2|253,2]];
- the way that leads to union with God consists in abnegation of self: [[349-toward-complete-purification#Meditation 1|349,1]];
- — of anything that may not be for the honor and glory of God: [[62-jesus-and-the-glory-of-the-father#Meditation 2|62,2]];
- — of any satisfaction and affection that does not lead to God: [[81-the-way-of-the-nothing#Meditation 1|81,1]];
- — of the satisfaction of taking account of one’s interior progress: [[115-to-be-hidden-from-myself#Meditation 1|115,1]];
- — of defending one’s rights: [[117-true-glory#Meditation 2|117,2]];
- — of one’s own will by means of obedience: [[120-the-value-of-obedience#Meditation 1|120,1]], [[120-the-value-of-obedience#Meditation 2|120,2]]; and fol.;
- — of one’s own views by acting according to God’s view as manifested by orders of superiors: [[124-blind-obedience#Meditation 1|124,1]];
- — of one’s own tastes and rights and ideas in favor of charity and fraternal union [[269-charity-adapts-itself-to-everyone#Meditation 1|269,1]], [[269-charity-adapts-itself-to-everyone#Meditation 2|269,2]]; [[308-fraternal-union#Meditation 1|308,1]], [[308-fraternal-union#Meditation 2|308,2]];
- — necessary for soul consecrated to God: [[76-correspondence-to-vocation#Meditation 1|76,1]].
