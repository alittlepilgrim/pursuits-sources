# Sanctifying Grace

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The grace which heals our human nature wounded by sin by giving us a share in the divine life of the Trinity. It is a habitual, supernatural gift which continues the work of sanctifying us--of making us "perfect," holy, and Christlike ([[3.1.3.2.p6z#^ccc-1999|1999]]).
