# Absolution

An essential element of the Sacrament of Penance in which the priest, by the power entrusted to the Church by Christ, pardons the sin(s) of the penitent.

([[2.2.2.4.p47|ccc-1424]], [[2.2.2.4.p4c|ccc-1442]] [[2.2.2.4.p4c|ccc-1449]], [[2.2.2.4.p4d|ccc-1453]], [[2.2.2.4.p4h|ccc-1480]]).
