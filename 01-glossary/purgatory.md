# Purgatory

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A state of final purification after death and before entrance into heaven for those who died in God's friendship, but were only imperfectly purified; a final cleansing of human imperfection before one is able to enter the joy of heaven ([[1.2.3.12.p2n#^ccc-1031|1031]]; cf. [[2.2.2.4.p4g#^ccc-1472|1472]]). ### -R-
