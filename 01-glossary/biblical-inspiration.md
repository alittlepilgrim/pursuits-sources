# Biblical Inspiration

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The gift of the Holy Spirit which assisted a human author to write a biblical book so that it has God as its author and teaches faithfully, without error, the saving truth that God has willed to be consigned to us ([[1.1.2.3.pp#^ccc-105|105]]).
