# Blasphemy

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Speech, thought, or action involving contempt for God or the Church, or persons or things dedicated to God. Blasphemy is directly opposed to the second commandment ([[3.2.1.2.p7i#^ccc-2148|2148]]).
