# Contraception, Artificial

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The use of mechanical, chemical, or medical procedures to prevent conception from taking place as a result of sexual intercourse; contraception offends against the openness to procreation required of marriage and also the inner truth of conjugal love ([[3.2.2.6.p86#^ccc-2370|2370]]).
