# Vicar of Christ

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A title given to St. Peter, head of the Twelve Apostles, and to his successors, the popes ([[1.2.3.9.p2a#^ccc-882|882]]); "vicar" means one who stands in for or acts for another.
