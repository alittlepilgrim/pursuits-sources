# Redeemer / Redemption

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Jesus Christ, redeemer of mankind. Christ paid the price of his own sacrificial death on the cross to ransom us, to set us free from the slavery of sin, thus achieving our redemption. ([[1.2.2.4.p1m#^ccc-571|571]], [[1.2.2.4.p1o#^ccc-601|601]]; cf. [[1.2.2.3.p1l#^ccc-517|517]], [[2.2.1.3.p41#^ccc-1372|1372]]).
