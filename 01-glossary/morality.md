# Morality

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Referring to the goodness or evil of human acts. Human freedom makes a person a "moral subject" or agent, able to judge the morality (goodness or evil) of the acts which are chosen. The morality of human acts depends on the object (or nature) of the action, the intention or end foreseen, and the circumstances of the action ([[3.1.1.4.p5q#^ccc-1749|1749]]; cf. [[1.2.1.1.p1c#^ccc-407|407]]).
