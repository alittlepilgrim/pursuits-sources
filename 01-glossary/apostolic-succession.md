# Apostolic Succession

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The handing on of apostolic preaching and authority from the Apostles to their successors the bishops through the laying on of hands, as a permanent office in the Church ([[1.1.2.2.pk|ccc-77]], [[1.2.3.9.p29|ccc-861]]).
