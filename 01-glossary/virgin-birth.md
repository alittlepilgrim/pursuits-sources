# Virgin Birth

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The conception of Jesus in the womb of the Virgin Mary solely by the power of the Holy Spirit. The Church's confession of faith in the virgin birth affirms that Jesus was conceived by the Holy Spirit without human seed ([[1.2.2.3.p1k#^ccc-496|496]]). _See_ Virgin Mary.
