# Seal of Confession

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The confessor's obligation to keep absolutely secret what a penitent has told to him in the Sacrament of Penance; also known as the "sacramental seal" ([[2.2.2.4.p4e#^ccc-1467|1467]]).
