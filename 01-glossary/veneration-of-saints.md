# Veneration of Saints

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Showing devotion and respect to Mary, the Apostles, and the martyrs, who were viewed as faithful witnesses to faith in Jesus Christ. Later, veneration was given to those who led a life of prayer and self-denial in giving witness to Christ, whose virtues were recognized and publicly proclaimed in their canonization as saints ([[1.2.3.9.p29#^ccc-828|828]]). Such veneration is often extended to the _relics_ or remains of those recognized as saints; indeed, to many sacred objects and _images_. Veneration must be clearly distinguished from [[adoration]] and worship, which are due to God alone ([[2.1.2.1.p38#^ccc-1154|1154]], [[2.2.4.1.p58#^ccc-1674|1674]], [[3.2.1.1.p7f#^ccc-2132|2132]]).
