# Hierarchy of Truths

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The order (hierarchy) of the truths in Catholic doctrine, insofar as they vary in their relation to the central mystery and foundation of Christian faith, the mystery of the Holy Trinity ([[1.1.2.2.pm#^ccc-90|90]], [[1.2.1.1.p17#^ccc-234|234]]).
