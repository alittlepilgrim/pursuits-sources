# Council, Ecumenical

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A gathering of all the bishops of the world, in the exercise of their collegial authority over the universal Church. An ecumenical council is usually called by the successor of St. Peter, the Pope, or at least confirmed or accepted by him ([[1.2.3.9.p2a#^ccc-884|884]]).
