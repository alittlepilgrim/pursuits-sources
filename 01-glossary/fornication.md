# Fornication

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Sexual intercourse between an unmarried man and an unmarried woman. Fornication is a serious violation of the sixth commandment of God ([[3.2.2.6.p85#^ccc-2353|2353]]).

## Ignatius Catholic Study Bible

**Fornication**

Among the "works of the flesh" condemned by Paul: Gal 5:19-21

Can lead to damnation: Eph 5:5; Heb 13:4; Rev 21:8; 22:15

Condemned by Jesus: Mt 15:19-20

Contrary to Christian discipleship: Eph 5:3-5; Col 3:5
