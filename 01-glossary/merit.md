# Merit

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The reward which God promises and gives to those who love him and by his grace perform good works. One cannot "merit" justification or eternal life, which are the free gift of God; the source of any merit we have before God is due to the grace of Christ in us ([[3.1.3.2.p70#^ccc-2006|2006]]).
