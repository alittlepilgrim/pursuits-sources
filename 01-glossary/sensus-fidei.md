# Sensus Fidei

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A supernatural appreciation of the faith (_sensus fidei_) shown by the universal consent in matters of faith and morals manifested by the whole body of the faithful under the guidance of the Magisterium ([[1.1.2.2.pm#^ccc-92|92]]).
