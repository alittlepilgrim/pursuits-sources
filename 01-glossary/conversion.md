# Conversion

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A radical reorientation of the whole life away from sin and evil, and toward God. This change of heart or conversion is a central element of Christ's preaching, of the Church's ministry of evangelization, and of the Sacrament of Penance and Reconciliation ([[2.2.2.4.p49#^ccc-1427|1427]], [[2.2.2.4.pp4a#^ccc-1431|1431]], [[2.2.2.4.p47#^ccc-1423|1423]]; cf. [[1.2.3.9.p29#^ccc-821|821]]).
