# Lord's Prayer

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The title early Christians gave to the prayer which Jesus entrusted to his disciples and to the Church (_Mt_6:9-13). This fundamental Christian prayer is also called the "Our Father," which are its first words ([[4.2.0.0.p9v#^ccc-2759|2759]]).
