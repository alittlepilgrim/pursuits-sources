# Definition, Dogmatic

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A solemn declaration by an ecumenical council or by the Pope that a doctrine is revealed by God and must be believed by the universal Church; such definitions are called infallible, and must be adhered to with the obedience of faith ([[1.2.3.9.p2a#^ccc-891|891]]).
