# Creation

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The act by which the eternal God gave a beginning to all that exists outside of himself. Creation also refers to the created universe or totality of what exists, as often expressed by the formula "the heavens and the earth" ([[1.2.1.1.p19#^ccc-290|290]]).
