# Inerrancy

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The attribute of the books of Scripture whereby they faithfully and without error teach that truth which God, for the sake of our salvation, wished to have confided through the Sacred Scriptures ([[1.1.2.3.pp#^ccc-107|107]]).
