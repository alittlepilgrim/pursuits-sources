# Bible (Glossary Definition)

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

[[scripture-sacred|Sacred Scripture]]: the books which contain the truth of God's [[revelation-definition|Revelation]] and were composed by human authors inspired by the [[holy-spirit|Holy Spirit]] (CCC [[1.1.2.3.pp|ccc-105]]). The Bible contains both the forty-six books of the [[old-testament|Old Testament]] and the twenty-seven books of the [[new-testament|New Testament]] (CCC [[1.1.2.3.pr|ccc-120]]).
