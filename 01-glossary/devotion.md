# Devotion

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- Absence of sensible — causes aridity: 153,1;
- consists in the promptness of the will in the service of God: 153,2; 282,2;
- is an act of the will and can subsist in spite of aridity and the rebellion of our lower nature: 153,2; 282,2;
- when it is deprived of relish, its value is doubled: 153,2; 282,2.
