# Reparation

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Making amends for a wrong done or for an offense, especially for sin, which is an offense against God. By his death on the cross, the Son of God offered his life out of love for the Father to make reparation for our sinful disobedience ([[1.2.2.4.p1o#^ccc-614|614]]). We are obliged to make reparation for personal sins against justice and truth, either through restitution of stolen goods or correcting the harm done to the other's good name. ([[3.2.2.7.p8b#^ccc-2412|2412]], [[3.2.2.8.p8k#^ccc-2487|2487]]). _See_ Satisfaction (for sin).
