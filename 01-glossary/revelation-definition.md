# Revelation (Definition)

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

God's communication of himself, by which he makes known the mystery of his divine plan, a gift of self-communication which is realized by deeds and words over time, and most fully by sending us his own divine Son, Jesus Christ ([[1.1.2.0.pe#^ccc-50|50]]).
