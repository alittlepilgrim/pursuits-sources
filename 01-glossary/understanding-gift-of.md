# Gift of Understanding

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- Enables one to penetrate the divine mysteries [[311-the-gift-of-understanding#Meditation 1|311,1]];
- introduces the soul to a prayer more simple and profound: [[311-the-gift-of-understanding#Meditation 2|311,2]];
- perfects the virtue of faith: [[311-the-gift-of-understanding#Meditation 1|311,1]], [[311-the-gift-of-understanding#Meditation 2|311,2]];
- purifies the mind of errors: [[312-blessed-are-the-clean-of-heart#Meditation 2|312,2]];
- to — corresponds the beatitude blessed are the clean of heart: [[312-blessed-are-the-clean-of-heart#Meditation 1|312,1]], [[312-blessed-are-the-clean-of-heart#Meditation 2|312,2]];
- purity of heart is necessary to become open to the influence of —: [[312-blessed-are-the-clean-of-heart#Meditation 1|312,1]];
- purity of heart is the fruit of this gift: [[312-blessed-are-the-clean-of-heart#Meditation 2|312,2]].
