# Marks of the Church

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The four attributes (marks or notes) of the Church mentioned in the Nicene-Constantinopolitan creed: "We believe in one, holy, catholic, and apostolic Church" ([[1.2.3.9.p29#^ccc-811|811]]).
