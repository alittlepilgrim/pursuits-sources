# Lectionary / Lector

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The official, liturgical book (_lectionary_) from which the reader (_lector_) proclaims the Scripture readings used in the Liturgy of the Word ([[2.1.2.1.p38#^ccc-1154|1154]]).
