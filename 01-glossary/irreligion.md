# Irreligion

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A vice contrary by defect to the virtue of religion. Irreligion directs us away from rendering to God what we as creatures owe him in justice ([[3.2.1.1.p7d#^ccc-2095|2095]], [[3.2.1.1.p7e#^ccc-2110|2110]]).
