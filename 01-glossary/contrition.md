# Contrition

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Sorrow of the soul and hatred for the sin committed, together with a resolution not to sin again. Contrition is the most important act of the penitent, and is necessary for the reception of the Sacrament of Penance ([[2.2.2.4.p4d#^ccc-1451|1451]]).
