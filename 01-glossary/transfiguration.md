# Transfiguration

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The mysterious event in which Jesus, seen speaking with Moses and Elijah on the mountain, was transformed in appearance--in the sight of Peter, James, and John --as a moment of disclosure of his divine glory ([[1.2.2.3.p1l#^ccc-554|554]]).
