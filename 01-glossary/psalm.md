# Psalm

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

A prayer in the Book of Psalms of the Old Testament, assembled over several centuries; a collection of prayers in the form of hymns or poetry. The psalms have been used since Jesus' time as the public prayer of the Church ([[4.1.1.1.p91#^ccc-2585|2585]]).
