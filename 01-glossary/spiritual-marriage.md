# Spiritual Marriage

## Divine Intimacy Meditations

- Is a total transformation in the Beloved [[367-perfect-union#Meditation 2|367,2]];
- is a union as perfect and full as is possible outside of the union of Heaven: [[367-perfect-union#Meditation 2|367,2]];
- love, fully uniting the soul to God, makes it so conformed to Him that it transforms it into Him: [[369-transforming-love#Meditation 1|369,1]];
- in its most elevated movements the soul adverts to the living God present and working within it: [[370-divine-intimacy#Meditation 1|370,1]];
- in the state of — the sensibility of the soul is fully pacified: [[367-perfect-union#Meditation 1|367,1]];
- the whole “capital” of the soul is employed in the practice of love: [[368-the-triumph-of-love#Meditation 1|368,1]];
- even the first motions are those of love: [[368-the-triumph-of-love#Meditation 1|368,1]];
- its sole activity consists in loving: [[368-the-triumph-of-love#Meditation 2|368,2]];
- it loves God with pure love: [[368-the-triumph-of-love#Meditation 2|368,2]];
- it loves God as it has been loved by Him because it loves Him with the Holy Spirit: [[369-transforming-love#Meditation 2|369,2]];
- it does not long for the to enjoy it, but to love God always more: [[370-divine-intimacy#Meditation 2|370,2]];
- it tends to produce works and to work: [[370-divine-intimacy#Meditation 2|370,2]];
- the glory souls attain in this state is in their power to assist their Crucified God by working for the salvation of souls: [[370-divine-intimacy#Meditation 2|370,2]].
