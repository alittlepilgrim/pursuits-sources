# Eyes of the Soul

## The Way of Perfection

EYES OF THE SOUL. The power of attending to, being aware of, or present to. Your eyes on your Spouse! ([[chapter-02-tj-way-ccel|2.1]]). All harm comes from not keeping our eyes fixed on the Lord ([[chapter-16-tj-way-ccel|16.11]]). The soul understands that it is close to God but not because it sees him with the eyes either of the body or soul ([[chapter-31-tj-way-ccel|31.2]]). Let us ask the Eternal Father that we might so receive our heavenly bread that the Lord may reveal himself to the eyes of our soul and make himself thereby known since our bodily eyes cannot delight in beholding him ([[chapter-34-tj-way-ccel|34.5]]).
