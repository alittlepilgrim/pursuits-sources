# Suicide

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The willful taking of one's own life; a grievous sin against the fifth commandment. A human person is neither the author nor the supreme arbiter of his life, of which God is sovereign master ([[3.2.2.5.p7z#^ccc-2280|2280]]).
