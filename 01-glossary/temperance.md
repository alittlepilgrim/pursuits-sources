# Temperance

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The cardinal moral virtue that moderates the attraction of pleasure and pro- vides balance in the use of created goods. It ensures the mastery of the will over instinct, and keeps natural desires within proper limits ([[3.1.1.7.p65#^ccc-1809|1809]]).
