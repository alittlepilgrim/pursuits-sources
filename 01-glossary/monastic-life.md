# Monastic Life

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Consecrated life marked by the public profession of religious vows of poverty, chastity, and obedience, and by a stable community life (in a monastery) with the celebration of the Liturgy of the Hours in choir (cf. [[1.2.3.9.p2a#^ccc-927|927]]).
