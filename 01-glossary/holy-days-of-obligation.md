# Holy Days of Obligation

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

Principal feast days on which, in addition to Sundays, Catholics are obliged by Church law to participate in the Eucharist; a precept of the Church ([[3.1.3.3.p75#^ccc-2043|2043]], [[3.2.1.3.p7o#^ccc-2180|2180]]).
