# Temptation

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

An attraction, either from outside oneself or from within, to act contrary to right reason and the commandments of God. Jesus himself during his life on earth was tempted, put to the test, to manifest both the opposition between himself and the devil and the triumph of his saving work over Satan ([[1.2.2.3.p1l#^ccc-538|538]]).

## Ignatius Catholic Study Bible

**Temptation**

Curse upon those who deliberately bring temptation to others: Mt 18:7; Lk 17:1

God does not tempt people: Jas 1:13

God limits the extent of temptation we face: 1 Cor 10:13

Jesus' overcoming of temptation: Mt 4:1-11; Mk 1:12-13; Lk 4:1-13; Heb 2:18; 4:15

Prayer to overcome temptation: Mt 6:13; 26:41; Mk 14:38; Lk 11:4; 22:40, 46; 1 Cor 7:5
