# Social Justice

## Catechism of the Catholic Church

The respect for the human person and the rights which flow from human dignity and guarantee it. Society must provide the conditions that allow people to obtain what is their due, according to their nature and vocation ([[3.1.2.3.p6n#^ccc-1928|1928]], [[3.1.2.3.p6o#^ccc-1931|1931]]).
