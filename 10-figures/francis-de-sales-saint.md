# Saint Francis de Sales

## Notes

- Introduction to the Devout Life.
- Treatise on the Love of God (Theotimus).
- Letters of spiritual direction, especially to St. Jane Frances de Chantal.
- Promoted gentle spirituality, pastoral reform during Reformation era.
