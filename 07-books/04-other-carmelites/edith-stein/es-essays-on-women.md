## Introduction

A collection of Edith Stein’s essays on the vocation and nature of women, written between the World Wars. These texts combine philosophy, theology, and spiritual reflection, and they position Stein as one of the most significant Catholic feminist thinkers of the 20th century.

Includes topical and place index.
