# The Manner of Lay Persons Baptizing an Infant, in Case of Danger of Death

Take common water, pour it on the head of the child, and while you are in the act of pouring it, say the following words:

 *I baptize thee, in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost. Amen.*

Note. Any person, whether man, woman, or child, may baptize an infant in case of danger of death.
