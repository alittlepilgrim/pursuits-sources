# Outline of the Second Letter of Saint Paul to Timothy

1. Opening Address (**1:1-2)**
2. Perseverance in Suffering (**1:3-2:13)**

A. Thanksgiving (1:3-5)

B. Admonitions for Timothy (1:6-14)

C. Update on Paul (1:15-18)

D. Personal Endurance (2:1-13)

3. Perseverance in Sound Doctrine (**2:14-4:8)**

A. Responsible Preaching (2:14-26)

B. Times of Distress (3:1-9)

C. Following Paul and Scripture (3:10-17)

D. The Mission of an Evangelist (4:1-8)

4. Conclusion (**4:9-22)**

A. Final Instructions and Warnings (4:9-18)

B. Greetings and Benediction (4:19-22)
