A. The Vocation to Virtue (1:3-11)

B. The Sure Prophetic Word (1:12-21)

3. Denunciation of False Teachers (**2:1-22)**

A. The Presence of Deceivers (2:1-3)

B. The Punishment of Deceivers (2:4-10)

C. The Poison of Deceivers (2:11-22)

4. The Promised Return of Christ (**3:1-17)**

A. The Skepticism of Scoffers (3:1-7)

B. The Day of the Lord (3:8-13)

C. Final Exhortation (3:14-17)

5. Closing Doxology (**3:18)**
