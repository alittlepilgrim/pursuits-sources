# Outline of the Letter of Saint Jude

1. Opening Address (**vv. 1-2)**
2. Initial Appeal (**vv. 3-4)**
3. Condemnation of False Teachers (**vv. 5-19)**

A. Three Biblical Warnings (vv. 5-8)

B. One Extrabiblical Warning (vv. 9-10)

C. Three Biblical Warnings (vv. 11-13)

D. One Extrabiblical Warning (vv. 14-16)

E. One Apostolic Prophecy (vv. 17-19)

4. Parting Appeal (**vv. 20-23)**
5. Closing Doxology (**vv. 24-25)**
