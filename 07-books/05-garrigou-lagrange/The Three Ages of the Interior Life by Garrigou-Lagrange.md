# The Three Ages of the Interior Life: Prelude of Eternal Life (1938)

## A Description of the Soul's Journey to Full Union with God

### By the Rev. Reginald Garrigou-lagrange, O.P

The book is summary of a course in ascetical and mystical theology given for over twenty years by Reginald Garrigou Lagrange, OP at the Angelicum in Rome.

In other words, this book describes in detail the path to the highest union with God possible in this life according to Catholic theology.
