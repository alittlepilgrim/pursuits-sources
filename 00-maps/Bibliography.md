Teresa of Ávila. *The Exclamations of the Soul to God: Or, the Meditations of St. Teresa After Communion*. Translated by John Milner. London: J. P. Coghlan, 1790.

## Classical Spiritual Masters

Bernard of Clairvaux. *The Twelve Degrees of Humility and of Pride*. Translated by Barton R.V. Mills, M.A. London: SPCK, 1929. Electronic edition prepared from the 1929 translation; translator's footnotes and introductory matter partially excised. Public domain.

Anonymous. *The Cloud of Unknowing*. Christian Classics Ethereal Library.

Catherine of Siena. *The Dialogue*. Translated and abridged by Algar Thorold. London: Kegan Paul, Trench, Trübner, 1907. Reprint, EWTN Library. [Abridged edition.]

Chautard, Dom Jean-Baptiste, O.C.S.O. *The Soul of the Apostolate*. Translated by a monk of Our Lady of Gethsemani. Trappist, KY: Abbey of Gethsemani, 1946.

Francis de Sales. *Introduction to the Devout Life*. Translated by John K. Ryan. New York: Doubleday, 1950. Reprint, Christian Classics Ethereal Library.

Francis de Sales. *Treatise on the Love of God*. Translated by Henry Benedict Mackey, O.S.B. Westminster, MD: Newman Press, 1953. Reprint, Christian Classics Ethereal Library.

Ignatius of Loyola. *The Spiritual Exercises of St. Ignatius*. Translated by Elder Mullan, S.J. New York: P.J. Kenedy & Sons, 1914. Reprint, Christian Classics Ethereal Library.

Kempis, Thomas à. *The Imitation of Christ*. Translated by William Benham. London: P.F. Collier & Son, 1909. Reprint, Christian Classics Ethereal Library.

Liguori, Alphonsus de. *Uniformity with God's Will*. Translated by Thomas W. Tobin, C.SS.R. Christian Classics Ethereal Library.

Rule of Saint Benedict. *The Holy Rule of St. Benedict*. Translated by Rev. Boniface Verheyen, O.S.B. Atchison, KS: Abbey Student Press, 1949. Reprint, Christian Classics Ethereal Library.
