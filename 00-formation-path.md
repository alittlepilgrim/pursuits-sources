
#  Formation Path (Carmelite Spiritual Theology)

This repository it is a **structured path of spiritual and doctrinal formation** within the Catholic tradition, centered on the Carmelite doctors and the classical theology of the interior life.

---

## How to Use This Library
    
- It is ordered according to the **three traditional stages of the spiritual life**
    
- Readers should begin with the **Purgative Way** and progress gradually
    

---

#  Scope

This collection is intentionally focused on:

- Carmelite spiritual theology
    
- Doctrinal fidelity to the Catholic Church
    
- The interior life and union with God
    

It is **not intended** to be:

- a comprehensive academic survey
    
- a representation of all theological perspectives
    
- or a modern scholarly bibliography
